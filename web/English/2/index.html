Karamel, a little cat, loved music very much.. Once upon a time, there was a curious little cat named Karamel who loved music. Karamel decided to go on an adventure across Turkey! She wanted to find all the special musical instruments played in different parts of the country. . Karamel traveled far and wide. She met people playing amazing instruments she had never seen before. In one place, she heard a stringed instrument that sounded like a gentle breeze. In another place, she saw a drum that made a strong, happy beat. Karamel listened carefully to each sound, learning about the history and stories behind every instrument. . She learned that each instrument was special to the people who played it. They used them to celebrate happy times, tell stories, and share their culture. Karamel had a wonderful time discovering all the beautiful music of Turkey. And she realized that music is a gift that brings everyone together!
