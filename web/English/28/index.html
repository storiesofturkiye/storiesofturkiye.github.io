Once upon a time, there was a curious little cat named Karamel who loved exploring different regions. One day, she decided to travel across the Marmara Region in Türkiye to learn about its unique culture, history, and natural beauty.

Istanbul: Hagia Sophia Karamel's first stop was Istanbul, where she visited the magnificent Hagia Sophia. The grand architecture and rich history of this iconic building fascinated her.

Bursa: Green Mosque and Silk Bazaar Next, Karamel traveled to Bursa, where she explored the beautiful Green Mosque and the bustling Silk Bazaar. She learned about the city's importance in the silk trade and its historical significance.

Edirne: Selimiye Mosque In Edirne, Karamel was amazed by the grandeur of the Selimiye Mosque. The impressive structure, designed by the famous architect Mimar Sinan, left her in awe.

Kocaeli: Kartepe Ski Resort Karamel's next stop was Kocaeli, where she experienced the excitement of skiing at Kartepe Ski Resort. The snowy slopes and winter fun made her day unforgettable.

17
Tekirdağ: Vineyards and Sunflower Fields In Tekirdağ, Karamel explored the beautiful vineyards and vast sunflower fields. The sight of endless sunflowers and grapevines was breathtaking.
