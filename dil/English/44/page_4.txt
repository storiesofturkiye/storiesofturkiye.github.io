Karamel, the adventurous little cat, wanted to learn about being kind to the Earth and living in a way that lasts. With excitement in her heart, she set off on a journey to explore different ways people in Türkiye are helping the planet. .
Karamel traveled to many places. She saw farmers using natural ways to grow food. They didn't use harsh chemicals, just things from nature! Karamel learned that this helps keep the soil healthy and the animals happy..
Next, she visited a town where people were collecting rainwater. They used it to water their gardens and wash their cars. This way, they didn't waste the precious water that falls from the sky. .
Karamel also saw a workshop where people were making things from recycled materials. Old plastic bottles became beautiful planters, and used fabric was turned into soft toys. It was amazing to see how things could be reused!.
She even visited a village where everyone composted their food scraps. The food waste was turned into rich soil to help grow new plants. This way, nothing went to waste! .
Karamel was so happy to see all the wonderful things people were doing to protect Türkiye's environment. She learned that even small actions can make a big difference. And she promised herself to do her part to be a good friend to the Earth.
