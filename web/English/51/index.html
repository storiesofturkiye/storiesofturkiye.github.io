Karamel, the curious cat, set out to explore the famous bridges of Türkiye. Each bridge had its own unique story, and Karamel was eager to learn about their history and architecture.. The first bridge Karamel visited was the Köprüçay Bridge. It was very old and strong. People had been crossing the river on this bridge for many, many years! Karamel looked up at the stone pillars and imagined all the people who had walked across before him. He wondered about the builders who made such a sturdy bridge so long ago.. Next, Karamel went to the Bosphorus Bridge. This bridge was very long and stretched over the water between two continents! Cars and trucks zoomed across it, and ships sailed underneath. Karamel watched them all go by, feeling the cool breeze from the sea. He thought about how important this bridge was for connecting people and places.. Finally, Karamel visited the Osmangazi Bridge. It was a beautiful bridge with tall towers. It was a busy place, full of people taking pictures and enjoying the view. Karamel sat on a warm stone and watched everything happening below. He felt happy to have explored all these amazing bridges and learned about their special stories. He knew there were many more bridges to see, and he couldn't wait for his next adventure!
