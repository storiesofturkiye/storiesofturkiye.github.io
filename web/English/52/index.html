She wanted to discover old ports, learn about big ships, and hear stories about sailors who sailed on the water. . She was very excited to learn all about the past adventures at the sea. Karamel thought it would be a wonderful journey filled with exciting tales! She started her adventure, ready to uncover the secrets hidden in the history of Türkiye’s coast.
