The Clever Girl in Burdur.
Karamel lived in Burdur, a sunny place by the sea. She was known as the cleverest girl around! People often told stories about how she used her smarts to solve problems. .
She had many amazing ideas. Sometimes, if someone needed help with something tricky, Karamel would think of a very clever way to fix it. She was always thinking and finding smart solutions. Everyone admired her cleverness and enjoyed listening to her stories about her clever adventures.
