Karamel was a very curious little cat. She loved to explore new places! One day, Karamel decided to go on a trip across the Black Sea Region in Türkiye. She wanted to learn all about the special culture, history, and beautiful nature of that area.
4
5
Rize: Tea Gardens.. Karamel’s first stop was Rize, a place with beautiful tea gardens. She went to see the many tea plants growing on the green hills. Seeing all that green was so refreshing and looked very pretty.
6
7
Trabzon: Sumela Monastery.. Next, Karamel traveled to Trabzon to see the ancient Sumela Monastery. The monastery was built right into the cliffs! It was a wonderful place to see, with amazing buildings and a long, long history.
8
9
Artvin: Karagöl Lake.. In Artvin, Karamel found a peaceful lake called Karagöl. The water in the lake was so clear and shiny, like a mirror! And all around the lake were green forests. It was a wonderful place to relax and feel calm.
10
11
Samsun: Bandırma Feribot Karamel then went to Samsun. She explored the old Bandırma Ferry. This ferry was very important in Turkey’s history, and Karamel really liked learning about how important it was.
12
13
Ordu: Boztepe Hill. In Ordu, Karamel loved looking at the beautiful sights from Boztepe hill. The view of the whole city and the Black Sea was amazing! It was so beautiful to see everything spread out below.
14
15
Sinop: Sinop Castle Karamel then traveled to Sinop, where she explored the ancient Sinop Castle. The castle's tall walls and the beautiful view of the Black Sea from the top were unforgettable.
16
17
Amasya: Yalıboyu Houses. In Amasya, Karamel loved looking at the beautiful Yalıboyu Houses beside the river. The houses were many different colors, and they looked so pretty when they were reflected in the water. It made a very lovely picture.

19
Karamel felt happy because she had so many new things to remember. She realized that the Black Sea Region was a very special and beautiful place. When she went home, she kept thinking about her next adventure.
