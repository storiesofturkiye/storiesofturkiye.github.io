Once upon a time, there was a curious little cat named Karamel who loved exploring different seasons. One day, she decided to travel across Turkey to experience the beauty and charm of fall.

Istanbul – Marmara Region: Karamel’s first stop in Istanbul was the Bosphorus, where she admired the stunning fall colors along its waters. The golden leaves and the crisp air made the city look magical.

Mediterranean Region – Antalya: Citrus Fruits in Fall In Antalya, Karamel discovered the abundance of fall citrus fruits. She enjoyed the fresh, juicy oranges and lemons, a delightful treat in the mild autumn weather.

Eastern Anatolia – Erzurum: Harvest Festivals In Erzurum, Karamel experienced the joy of harvest festivals. She enjoyed traditional music, dance, and the festive atmosphere that celebrated the season.
