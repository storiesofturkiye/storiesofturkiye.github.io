Karakter yang ingin belajar tentang budaya keluarga yang berbeza di tujuh wilayah Türkiye. Dengan semangat yang tinggi dan peta di dalam cakarnya, dia memulai petualangan baru untuk mengunjungi keluarga di setiap wilayah.. ---. Once upon a time, Karamel, the curious little cat, wanted to learn about the diverse family cultures across the seven regions of Türkiye. With excitement in her heart and a map in her paw, she set off on a new adventure to visit families in each region. She wanted to see how people lived, what they ate, and how they celebrated together.. First, Karamel traveled to the Black Sea region. She met a family who loved to fish and make delicious meals with fresh seafood. They sang songs about the sea and told stories about brave sailors. Karamel learned that families in this region were very kind and welcoming.. Next, she went to the Marmara region. Here, she found a family who ran a bustling market. They sold all sorts of colorful fruits, vegetables, and spices! The family was very busy, but they always had time for a warm smile and a friendly chat with their customers. Karamel learned that families in this region were hardworking and cheerful.. Then, Karamel journeyed to the Aegean region. She visited a family who grew grapes and made sweet wine. They had beautiful vineyards and enjoyed spending time outdoors. The family loved to play games and tell jokes. Karamel learned that families in this region were relaxed and happy.. After that, she traveled to the Mediterranean region. Here, she met a family who lived by the sea and loved to swim and sunbathe. They ate fresh fish and drank cool watermelons. The family was very friendly and inviting. Karamel learned that families in this region were lively and joyful.. Next, Karamel went to the Central Anatolia region. She visited a family who lived on a farm and raised sheep and cows. They made delicious cheese and yogurt. The family was very kind and generous. Karamel learned that families in this region were strong and helpful.. Then, she traveled to the Eastern Anatolia region. Here, she met a family who lived in a cozy village surrounded by mountains. They told stories about ancient legends and played traditional music. The family was very warm and welcoming. Karamel learned that families in this region were proud of their heritage and loved to share their traditions.. Finally, Karamel arrived in the Southeastern Anatolia region. She met a family who lived in a beautiful garden with lots of flowers and trees. They made delicious desserts and drank fragrant tea. The family was very hospitable and kind. Karamel learned that families in this region were generous and loved to welcome guests.. Karamel had a wonderful time visiting all the families and learning about their different cultures. She realized that even though people lived in different regions, they all shared the same love for family and friendship. And she made many new friends along the way! She returned home with a heart full of happiness and stories to tell.
