Once upon a time, there was a playful little cat named Karamel. One day, she decided to travel across Türkiye to learn about the delicious Turkish treats..
Karamel started her journey in Istanbul. She saw the beautiful Hagia Sophia and the Blue Mosque. She smelled the yummy spices in the Spice Market. .
Next, Karamel traveled to Cappadocia. She saw the fairy chimneys and rode in a hot air balloon. It was like floating in the clouds!.
Then, she went to Pamukkale. She saw the white terraces made of calcium. She dipped her paws in the warm, clear water..
Karamel visited Ephesus, an ancient city with amazing ruins. She imagined people walking there a long, long time ago. .
She tasted baklava in Gaziantep and Turkish delight in Bursa. Everything was so delicious! Karamel learned that Türkiye had many beautiful places and tasty foods.  She was very happy to have explored her country.
