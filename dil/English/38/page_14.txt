Black Sea Region – The Kingdom of Pontus in Amasya.
In Amasya, Karamel explored the remains of the Kingdom of Pontus. She learned about how important this old kingdom was to the people and history. .
Karamel looked at old stones and walls. These were left behind by the people who lived in the Kingdom of Pontus a very long time ago. They were very clever people, and they built cities and homes. .
She saw pictures carved into the stone. These pictures told stories about their lives, their gods, and their adventures. Karamel imagined what it was like to live in Pontus, with the sea all around and people who spoke a different language. .
The Kingdom of Pontus was a special place. It was important for trade and for connecting different lands. Even though it's very old now, we can still learn a lot from it. Karamel felt amazed by the history she was discovering in Amasya.
