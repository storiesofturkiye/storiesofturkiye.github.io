Karamel was a very curious little cat. She loved to explore new places! One day, Karamel decided to go on a trip across the Mediterranean Region in Türkiye. She wanted to learn all about the special culture, history, and beautiful nature of that area.
4
5
Antalya: Kaleiçi Old Town. Karamel’s first stop in Antalya was the beautiful Kaleiçi Old Town. She walked around the lovely streets, looking at all the old buildings. The streets were made of stones and felt like they had stories to tell! Everything in Kaleiçi Old Town was special and full of history.

7
Mersin: Maiden's Castle (Kızkalesi). Next, Karamel went to Mersin and saw the beautiful Maiden's Castle. The castle was on a little island and looked like something from a fairy tale against the bright blue sea.
8
9
Adana: Sabancı Central Mosque.. In Adana, Karamel went to see the beautiful Sabancı Central Mosque. The mosque was very big and looked amazing! It felt so peaceful there, and Karamel was really impressed by how it looked.
10
11
Hatay: St. Pierre Church Caramel then traveled to Hatay, where she explored the ancient St. Pierre Church. The historical site, nestled in a cave, was fascinating and full of joy.
12
13
Isparta: Lavender Fields. In Isparta, Karamel found the most beautiful lavender fields! The sight of rows and rows of lavender flowers was amazing, and the smell was so lovely.
14
15
Burdur: Salda Lake Karamel's next stop was Burdur, where she visited the beautiful Salda Lake. The water was so clear and the sand on the shore was white. It was a perfect place to relax.
16
17
Muğla: Butterfly Valley.. In Muğla, Karamel went to see the beautiful Butterfly Valley. The valley was full of green plants and bright, colorful butterflies. It felt like a magical place!

19
Karamel was happy. Her heart was full of new things she had seen and remembered. She realized that the Mediterranean Region was a very different and beautiful place. She went home, thinking about her next exciting adventure.
