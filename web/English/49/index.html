Karamel, the curious little cat, wanted to explore the beautiful and magical gardens of Türkiye. She decided to visit famous gardens across the country to learn about the plants and history of each garden. With excitement in her heart, she set off on a new adventure.

Gülhane Park - Istanbul. Karamel’s first stop in Istanbul was Gülhane Park. She looked at the beautiful roses and the old trees. She liked the quiet feeling of this park, which is one of the oldest parks in the city.

Atatürk Arboretum - Istanbul. Next, Karamel went to the Atatürk Arboretum in Istanbul. She looked around at all the different kinds of trees and plants. She learned how important it is to protect nature.

Emirgan Park - Istanbul. In Emirgan Park, Karamel was amazed by the colorful tulips during the annual Tulip Festival. She looked at the flower beds, which were arranged so beautifully, and all the bright colors.

11
Yıldız Park - Istanbul. Karamel then visited Yıldız Park, a big old park with beautiful gardens and lovely views of the Bosphorus. She enjoyed a peaceful walk through the park, looking at all the different plants and flowers.

13
Karaca Arboretum - Yalova. In Yalova, Karamel visited the Karaca Arboretum. This place is special because it has lots and lots of plants and trees from all over the world! She learned about how people are working hard to protect plants and animals that are rare and might disappear if we don't help them.

Çukurova University Botanical Garden - Adana. In Adana, Karamel went to the Çukurova University Botanical Garden. It was a special garden where scientists studied many different kinds of plants! She learned about all the interesting things they were researching and why it’s important to protect plants.

17
Dilek Peninsula-Büyük Menderes Delta National Park - Aydın. Finally, Karamel explored the Dilek Peninsula-Büyük Menderes Delta National Park in Aydın. She was amazed by how beautiful the park was, with so many different plants and animals.

Karamel returned home with a heart full of wonder and a mind filled with the magical gardens of Türkiye. She thought about all the beautiful flowers and plants she had seen..
