Once upon a time, there was a playful little cat named Karamel. One day, she decided to travel across Türkiye to learn about the delicious Turkish delights.. Karamel started her journey in Istanbul. She saw the beautiful Hagia Sophia and the Blue Mosque. She met many friendly people who offered her tasty treats. . Next, Karamel traveled to Cappadocia. She was amazed by the fairy chimneys and the colorful balloons flying in the sky. She learned about the history of the cave dwellings where people used to live.. After Cappadocia, Karamel went to Ephesus. She explored the ancient ruins of the city and imagined what life was like thousands of years ago. She saw the Library of Celsus and the Great Theatre.. Karamel continued her journey to Pamukkale. She was fascinated by the white terraces formed by the mineral-rich water. She dipped her paws in the warm, clear pools and had lots of fun.. Finally, Karamel arrived in Antalya. She explored the beautiful beaches and the ancient harbour. She saw the Roman ruins and enjoyed the sunshine. . Karamel learned so much about Türkiye during her travels. She tasted delicious Turkish delights, saw amazing sights, and met many kind people. She had a wonderful adventure!

## Frugtbullar fra Antalya. I Middelhavsområdet, i Antalya, er der noget meget lækkert. Det er farverige spyd med friske frugter! De er både gode for dig og meget velsmagende. . Frugtbullerne er lavet af forskellige slags frugt, som for eksempel jordbær, ananas, melon og druer. Frugterne er skåret i små stykker og stukket på et spyd. . Det er en populær snack i Antalya, og mange mennesker nyder at spise dem. De er perfekte til en varm dag eller som en sund godbid. Frugtbullerne er nemme at spise og fylder ikke maven for meget.. Hvis du besøger Antalya, skal du prøve disse lækre frugtbullar! Du vil ikke blive skuffet.
