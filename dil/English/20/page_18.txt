Eastern Anatolia - Erzurum: Milk Halva.
Once upon a time, in a sunny place called Erzurum, there was a very special treat: milk halva..
This milk halva was made with love. First, milk was heated gently. Then, flour and sugar were added and stirred carefully. The mixture cooked slowly until it became thick and golden brown. .
It was a labor of love, made with patience.  The people of Erzurum had a special way of making this delicious treat. They followed traditions passed down through many years. It was a recipe filled with care and warmth..
Everyone loved to eat milk halva! It was perfect for celebrations, or just a sweet snack.  It tasted like sunshine and happiness. It warmed you from the inside out. .
The milk halva of Erzurum wasn't just food; it was a piece of their history, a symbol of their kindness, and a reminder of home.  It brought smiles to everyone who tasted it. And that made everyone very happy indeed.
