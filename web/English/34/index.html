Once upon a time, in a sunny corner of the world called Türkiye, there lived a curious little cat named Karamel. Karamel loved adventures and animals. One day, she found a colorful map with sparkling paths that led to Türkiye's unique animals and their habitats. Excited, Karamel set off to meet these wonderful creatures.

Marmara Region - Dolphins in the Bosphorus, Istanbul Karamel's first stop was Istanbul, where she sailed along the Bosphorus Strait. Here, she met playful dolphins jumping in and out of the water, showing off their acrobatics and racing alongside boats.

Aegean Region - Olive Ridley Sea Turtles in Muğla Story Next, Karamel traveled to the beautiful beaches of Muğla, where she met olive ridley sea turtles. These gentle creatures were busy laying their eggs in the warm sand, preparing for the arrival of their tiny hatchlings.

Mediterranean Region - Caretta Caretta Turtles in Antalya In Antalya, Karamel encountered the majestic caretta caretta turtles. She watched in awe as these large turtles gracefully swam through the clear waters and nested on the sandy shores.

Central Anatolia – Wild Horses in Cappadocia In the heart of Türkiye, Karamel visited the stunning region of Cappadocia. Here, she saw wild horses running freely across the vast, rocky landscapes, their manes flowing in the wind.

Black Sea Region – Migratory Birds in the Kızılırmak Delta Karamel then traveled to the Kızılırmak Delta in the Black Sea region. She was amazed by the variety of migratory birds stopping to rest and feed, creating a vibrant and lively atmosphere.

Eastern Anatolia – Van Cats in Van: In Eastern Anatolia, Karamel visited Van and met the famous Van cats. These beautiful cats, with their mismatched eyes, were friendly and playful, delighting Karamel with their antics.

Southeastern Anatolia – Gazelles in Şanlıurfa Karamel's final stop was Şanlıurfa, where she met graceful gazelles. She watched them elegantly move across the plains, their slender bodies and swift movements leaving her in awe.
