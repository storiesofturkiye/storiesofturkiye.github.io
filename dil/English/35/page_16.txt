## The Treasure of Mount Ağrı.
In Eastern Anatolia, a brave girl named Karamel went to Mount Ağrı. She had heard a story about Noah's Ark, the big boat that floated on the water long, long ago. .
Karamel was curious and wanted to see if the story was true. While exploring the mountain, she found something amazing! It was hidden treasures. People believed these treasures were left behind from Noah's Ark. .
She carefully looked at each item, wondering about the people who might have used them so many years ago. The treasures were a special reminder of a very old story and a great journey.
