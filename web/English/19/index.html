Karamel, a playful little cat, loved playing games and doing fun things. One day, she decided to visit playgrounds all over Türkiye. She wanted to learn about the games that children play in Türkiye.. She went to a playground in Istanbul. She saw children playing a game with a ball. They were kicking the ball and laughing! Karamel watched them carefully.. Next, she traveled to a playground in Ankara. There, she saw children playing a game with sticks. They were trying to balance the sticks on their feet! It looked very tricky.. After that, Karamel went to a playground in İzmir. She saw children playing a game where they were running and chasing each other. They were shouting and having so much fun!. Karamel learned lots of new games during her trip. She had a wonderful time exploring different playgrounds and seeing how children play in Türkiye. She was happy to have learned all about the traditional games.
