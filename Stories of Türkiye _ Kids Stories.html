Start Discovering the Stories of Türkiye

Welcome to our website, where you can explore the rich culture and history of Türkiye through engaging and educational audiobooks. Our collection of stories introduces the unique traditions, historical landmarks, and vibrant regions of Türkiye, designed to captivate young learners aged 4-10. Dive into the adventures of Karamel the Cat as it explores Türkiye, from the bustling streets of Istanbul to the serene landscapes of Cappadocia. Through our audiobooks, children can enjoy fascinating tales while gaining a deeper appreciation for Turkish culture and heritage.

Join us on this exciting journey to learn about the wonders of Türkiye!
