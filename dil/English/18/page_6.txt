The Conquest of Constantinople.
In the Marmara Region – Istanbul, there is a story about a very important event called the Conquest of Constantinople. This happened in 1453. A strong and clever leader named Fatih, also known as the Conqueror, led the people to take control of the city. .
Fatih was a brave person. He planned carefully and fought hard. He wanted to make Constantinople a new and important place. The conquest of Constantinople was a big change in history. It marked the end of the old empire and the beginning of a new era. People remember Fatih as a great leader who changed the world.
