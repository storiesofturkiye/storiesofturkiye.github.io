Mediterranean Region - Antalya: The Tale of the Chimera..
In Antalya, Karamel heard a story about the Chimera. The Chimera was a creature from old legends – a very special and amazing animal that could breathe fire! Karamel went to a place where you can still see flames coming from the rocks. These flames are like a reminder of the old story about the Chimera. It shows that the legend might have been true!
