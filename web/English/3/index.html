Once upon a time, there was a curious little cat named Karamel who loved to explore and celebrate. Karamel set off on a journey across Türkiye to discover the unique holidays and festivals in each region.

Marmara Region - Istanbul: Republic Day Karamel's first stop was Istanbul, where she joined the Republic Day celebrations. The streets were filled with people waving flags, singing songs, and watching fireworks.

Aegean Region - Izmir: Atatürk's Memorial Day Next, Karamel traveled to Izmir to witness Atatürk's Memorial Day. People gathered at Atatürk's monument to lay flowers and remember the founder of modern Türkiye.

Mediterranean Region - Antalya: Hıdırellez In Antalya, Karamel joined the Hıdırellez festivities. People danced around bonfires, jumped over flames, and celebrated the arrival of spring.

Central Anatolia - Ankara: Children’s Day In Ankara, Karamel took part in Children’s Day celebrations. The city was filled with children performing dances, singing songs, and enjoying various activities.

13
Black Sea Region - Rize: Tea Festival Karamel traveled to Rize in the Black Sea region for the Tea Festival. People celebrated the tea harvest with music, dance, and tea-tasting events.

15
Eastern Anatolia - Erzurum: Winter Festival In Erzurum, Karamel experienced the Winter Festival. People enjoyed ice skating, skiing, and snowball fights in the snowy mountains.

17
Southeastern Anatolia - Gaziantep: Pistachio Festival Karamel's final stop was Gaziantep for the Pistachio Festival. The city was filled with delicious pistachio treats, music, and dancing.
