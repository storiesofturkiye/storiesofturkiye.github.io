Black Sea Region - Trabzon: Silver Filigree Karamel then traveled to Trabzon, where she learned about the delicate art of silver filigree. Artisans twisted and braided fine silver wires into intricate designs, creating beautiful jewelry. .
They made tiny flowers, swirling patterns, and sparkling shapes. It took a lot of patience and skill to make each piece. The silver wires were so thin, like strands of hair! The artisans worked carefully, shaping the silver with tiny tools. They created necklaces, earrings, and bracelets that were truly works of art. Silver Filigree Karamel was amazed by how beautiful and detailed the jewelry was. She practiced twisting and braiding the silver, trying her best to create her own designs. It was hard work, but she loved learning this special skill in Trabzon.
