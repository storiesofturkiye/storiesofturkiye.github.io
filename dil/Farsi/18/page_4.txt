**کامیاب شدن Karamel و تاریخ**.
Once upon a time, there was a curious little cat named Karamel who loved learning about history. One day, she decided to explore the rich history of the ancient city of Persepolis. .
Karamel carefully walked through the ruins. She saw big stone walls and old carvings on them.  She read about kings and queens who lived there a long, long time ago. It was so interesting!.
Suddenly, Karamel spotted something shiny under a pile of stones.  She carefully moved the stones and found an old clay tablet. The tablet had pictures and strange symbols on it. .
Karamel spent a long time trying to understand the tablet. She remembered what she had learned in her history books. Slowly, she started to figure out what the pictures meant. They told stories about the people who built Persepolis and how they lived..
Karamel was so happy! She had discovered something amazing and learned even more about the past.  She knew that history was full of secrets waiting to be found. And she couldn't wait to explore more!.
