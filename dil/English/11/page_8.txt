Aegean Region - Manisa: Keloğlan Next, Karamel traveled to Manisa, where she met Keloğlan, the clever and bald-headed hero. Keloğlan shared his adventurous stories and how he always found a way to solve problems. .
Keloğlan was very happy to see Karamel. He told her about all of his exciting adventures. Once, he helped a poor farmer who was being tricked by a greedy merchant. Keloğlan used his cleverness to trick the merchant and help the farmer get his land back! .
Another time, Keloğlan rescued a princess from a scary dragon. He didn't use a sword or a shield. Instead, he used his smarts to outsmart the dragon and save the day. .
Karamel listened carefully to every story. She was amazed by how brave and clever Keloğlan was. He always knew what to do, even when things seemed impossible. Keloğlan showed Karamel that being smart is the best way to solve any problem.
