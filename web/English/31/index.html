Karamel was a very curious little cat. She loved to explore new places! One day, Karamel decided to go on a trip across the Eastern Anatolia Region in Türkiye. She wanted to learn all about the special culture, history, and beautiful nature of that area.

Van.. Lake Van Karamel’s first stop was Van, where she really liked seeing Lake Van. The water was so clear, and the mountains behind it looked amazing. It was a perfect place to relax.

Erzurum: Çifte Minareli Medrese.. Karamel went to Erzurum. She visited the old Çifte Minareli Medrese. The Medrese was very impressive! Karamel loved the beautiful buildings and all the detailed designs. They made her feel amazed.

Ağrı: Mount Ağrı. In Ağrı, Karamel saw the majestic Mount Ağrı. The towering peak covered in snow was a breathtaking sight.

Kars: Caramel went to Kars. She visited the old Ani Ruins. These ruins are very old and tell stories about a long time ago. The ruins have ancient churches and strong city walls. They show us what life was like in the past.

13
Malatya: Apricot Orchards. In Malatya, Karamel found the famous apricot orchards. The sight of all the ripe apricots hanging from the trees was beautiful. And the sweet smell of the apricots was wonderful!

15
Elazığ: Harput Castle. Karamel's next adventure took her to a city called Elazığ, where she visited the old Harput Castle. The castle was very important in history, and the view from the top of its walls was amazing!

17
Munzur Valley National Park in Tunceli. In Tunceli, Karamel went for a hike in the Munzur Valley National Park. The park was full of green plants, rivers flowing gently, and many different animals. It was a wonderful place for an exciting adventure!

Karamel's heart was happy with all the new things she had seen and all the memories she made. She understood how different and interesting the Eastern Anatolia Region is. When she went home, she kept thinking about her next exciting adventure!
