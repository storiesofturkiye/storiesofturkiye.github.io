Eastern Anatolia - Kemençe Music in Erzurum.
Karamel went on a trip to Erzurum. In Erzurum, she heard the beautiful sounds of a Kemençe. The Kemençe is a stringed instrument, and the music it made was very moving. It felt like a sad and happy story all at once! Karamel really loved the music. It helped her understand how old and special the traditions of this place are.
