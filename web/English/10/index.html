Marmara Region - Istanbul: Hagia Sophia Karamel's first stop was in Istanbul, where she visited the magnificent Hagia Sophia. She looked in wonder at its big dome and beautiful pictures made of tiles. She learned about its history – it was both a church and a mosque at different times.

Aegean Region - Izmir: Ephesus. Next, Karamel traveled to Izmir to visit the ancient city of Ephesus. She explored the grand Library of Celsus and the Great Theatre, imagining the bustling life of this ancient city.

Aspendos Theatre in Antalya.. In Antalya, Karamel went to see the old Aspendos Theatre. She was amazed by how well it was kept and imagined all the plays and events that happened there a long, long time ago.

Göreme Open Air Museum in Cappadocia.. In Cappadocia, Karamel went to see the Göreme Open Air Museum. She walked around the churches and monasteries that were carved into the rocks. Karamel was very amazed by the old pictures painted on the walls.

Sumela Monastery.. Karamel traveled to Trabzon in the Black Sea region. There, she went to see the Sumela Monastery. The monastery was built on top of a very high cliff! Karamel looked around and saw amazing views. She also looked closely at all the beautiful and detailed parts of the monastery. It was very impressive.

Eastern Anatolia - Ani Ruins.. In Eastern Anatolia, Karamel went to the old city of Ani. People also call it the "City of 1001 Churches"! She walked around the ruins and learned about how important this city was a long, long time ago.

Southeastern Anatolia - Mount Nemrut.. Karamel’s journey ended at Mount Nemrut. There, she found huge statues and a very old tomb belonging to King Antiochus I. Karamel was really amazed by how grand and impressive the place looked.
