Mediterranean Region - Antalya: Water Sports In Antalya.
In Antalya, Karamel found something really exciting: water sports! She tried lots of fun things, like riding a jet ski – it was super fast! And she went parasailing, which means she floated up in the air with a big parachute. It felt amazing to see the beautiful coastline from way up high. She loved the rush of excitement and the lovely sea all around.
