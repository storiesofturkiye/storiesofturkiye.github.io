Çemberlitaş Next, Karamel visited Çemberlitaş, known for the Column of Constantine. She admired the ancient column standing tall in the bustling area filled with history. .
The column was very old, and it had stood there for many, many years! People from a long time ago had built it. It was so big and strong. Karamel looked up at the top of the column and imagined all the people who had seen it before her. It made her feel like she was stepping back in time. The area around the column was always busy with people walking around, shopping, and talking. It was a very special place to visit.
