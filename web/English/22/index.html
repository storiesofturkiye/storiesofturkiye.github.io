Karamel was a curious little cat. She loved exploring all the different seasons. One day, Karamel decided to travel all across Türkiye. She wanted to see the beautiful things that happened in the fall.. The leaves on the trees were changing colors – red, yellow, and orange! Karamel watched them fall gently to the ground. She chased the leaves and played hide-and-seek among them. It was so much fun! . She saw pumpkins everywhere! Big pumpkins and small pumpkins. Some were orange, some were white. Karamel even saw people carving faces into the pumpkins. . The air smelled like cinnamon and apples. People were baking yummy treats. Karamel sniffed the air happily. She wished she could have a little taste!. Karamel walked through fields of corn. The corn stalks were tall and dry. She saw farmers collecting the corn for the winter. It was a busy time of year. . As the days got shorter, the sun set earlier. The sky turned a beautiful shade of purple and pink. Karamel curled up in a cozy spot, watching the stars come out. Fall was a magical time of year, and Karamel loved every minute of it.

Marmara Region - Istanbul: Bosphorus in Fall. Karamel’s first stop was Istanbul. She really liked Istanbul because she saw the beautiful fall colors along the Bosphorus. The leaves were golden, and the air felt nice and cool. It made the whole city look like a fairy tale!

Aegean Region - Izmir: Olive Harvest. Next, Karamel traveled to Izmir, where she joined the olive harvest. She loved picking olives and learning about how they are turned into delicious olive oil. . Karamel carefully walked through the olive trees. The trees were heavy with green and black olives! She gently touched the leaves and looked up at the branches full of fruit.. The people working in the olive grove used special tools to knock the olives off the branches. They gathered the olives in big baskets. Karamel helped too, picking up any olives that fell on the ground. . She watched as the olives were taken to a special place. There, they were washed and crushed. The crushed olives turned into a thick, green liquid – olive oil! It smelled so wonderful.. Karamel learned that olive oil is used to make yummy food and it's very healthy for you. She was so happy to have helped with the olive harvest and learn about this special oil.

Fall Festivals in Erzurum. In Eastern Anatolia, in a city called Erzurum, Karamel was so happy! It was fall, and there were lots of fun festivals happening. . Everyone was smiling and having a wonderful time celebrating the fall season. It was a very festive and joyful day for Karamel.
