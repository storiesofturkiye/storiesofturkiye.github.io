Karamel, a curious little cat, loved to explore new places. One day, she decided to travel through the Marmara Region in Türkiye. She wanted to learn about the special culture, history, and beautiful nature of that area.
4
5
Istanbul: Hagia Sophia Karamel’s first stop was Istanbul, where she visited the magnificent Hagia Sophia. The grand architecture and rich history of this iconic building fascinated her.
6
7
Bursa: Green Mosque and Silk Bazaar.. Next, Karamel traveled to Bursa. She explored the beautiful Green Mosque and the busy Silk Bazaar. She learned that Bursa was a very important city for making silk. It also has a long and interesting history.
8
9
Edirne: Selimiye Mosque.. In Edirne, Karamel was amazed by the grand Selimiye Mosque. The beautiful building, designed by the famous architect Mimar Sinan, made her feel very impressed.

11
Çanakkale: Gallipoli and Trojan Horse. Karamel went to Çanakkale for a trip. She visited the old Gallipoli Peninsula and saw the famous Trojan Horse! She learned about the very important things that happened during World War I there. She also heard the amazing story of Troy, a long time ago.
12
13
Cunda Island in Balıkesir. In Balıkesir, Karamel went to the lovely island of Cunda. She really liked how beautiful it was and how peaceful it felt. It was a perfect place for her to relax and have fun!
14
15
Kocaeli: Kartepe Ski Resort. Karamel's next adventure took her to Kocaeli, to the beautiful Kartepe Ski Resort! She had so much fun skiing down the snowy hills. The snow was sparkling, and it was a very exciting day for her. She will never forget her time at Kartepe Ski Resort.
16
17
Tekirdağ: Vineyards and Sunflower Fields. In Tekirdağ, Karamel went to see the beautiful vineyards and huge fields of sunflowers. Seeing all those sunflowers and grape vines stretching as far as he could see was amazing!

19
Karamel felt happy, thinking about all the new things she had seen and all the wonderful memories she made. She realized that the Marmara Region was a very special place with lots of different things to see! When she went home, she dreamed about her next exciting adventure.
