Karamel's Journey. Once upon a time, there was a curious little cat named Karamel who loved to explore nature. Karamel decided to go on a trip all across Türkiye! She wanted to see the beautiful landscapes and amazing natural wonders in every part of the country.

Marmara Region - Istanbul: The Bosphorus Karamel's first stop was Istanbul, where she admired how beautiful the Bosphorus was. She went on a boat trip and enjoyed seeing the city's buildings and the calm water.
6
7
Pamukkale in the Aegean Region.. Next, Karamel traveled to Pamukkale in the Aegean region. She was very amazed by the white terraces and the warm thermal waters. It felt like walking on clouds!
8
9
Düden Waterfalls in Antalya. In Antalya, Karamel went to see the Düden Waterfalls. The sound of the water falling down and all the green plants around made it a really nice place for a relaxing day.

11
Central Anatolia - Cappadocia.. In Central Anatolia, Karamel explored Cappadocia. She was very amazed by the fairy chimneys. They looked like tall, strange people! Karamel even went on a hot air balloon ride. It was so much fun to see the beautiful land below from way up high.

13
Uzungöl in the Black Sea Region.. Uzungöl in the Black Sea region is a very beautiful place. Karamel from the Black Sea region went to Uzungöl. The lake is surrounded by green mountains, and there are traditional wooden houses. It looks just like a beautiful painting!
14
15
Eastern Anatolia - Mount Ağrı.. In Eastern Anatolia, Karamel went to Mount Ağrı. Mount Ağrı is the tallest mountain in Türkiye! The mountain has snow on top, and there's a story about Noah's Ark there. This makes Mount Ağrı seem like a very magical place.

17
Göbeklitepe.. Southeastern Anatolia - Göbeklitepe Karamel’s final stop was Göbeklitepe in Southeastern Anatolia. She explored the ancient ruins and learned about the world's oldest known temple.

19
Karamel felt amazed and remembered many things. She realized that Turkey’s real treasures were its beautiful nature and all the stories it held. She went home, thinking about her next exciting trip.
