The Stone Bridge Karamel then traveled to Adana to see the ancient Stone Bridge. Built by the Romans, the bridge had stood the test of time. Karamel was amazed by its durability and the stories it held. .
The Stone Bridge was very old. It had been built a long, long time ago by people called Romans. The bridge was strong and sturdy. It had been standing there for many years, even centuries! .
Karamel looked up at the Stone Bridge with big eyes. She was amazed! The bridge looked so strong. Karamel imagined all the things the bridge had seen. She thought about all the people who had walked across it before her. The Stone Bridge held so many stories from the past. It was wonderful to see.
