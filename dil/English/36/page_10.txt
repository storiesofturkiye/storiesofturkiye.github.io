Mediterranean Region – Marine Biology Research in Antalya.
In Antalya, Karamel dove into the underwater world with marine biologists. She discovered the vibrant marine life and learned about the importance of protecting the ocean. .
Karamel was so excited! She got to go diving in the sea near Antalya. She went with some people who study sea creatures – they are called marine biologists. .
When Karamel swam underwater, she saw so many amazing things! Colorful fish swam past her. She saw corals that looked like plants and sea turtles gliding gracefully. There were all sorts of interesting animals living in the ocean. .
The marine biologists showed Karamel how important it is to take care of the ocean. They explained that the ocean is home to many creatures, and we need to keep it clean and healthy so they can all live happily. Karamel learned that even small things, like not throwing trash into the sea, can help protect the ocean. She was happy to be part of helping the ocean!
