Antalya Bazaar.
In Antalya, Karamel and her family visited the local bazaar. It was a very colorful and lively place! Karamel saw beautiful carpets that were made by hand, and also traditional Turkish pottery. .
Karamel was amazed by the carpets. They had very detailed designs, and she could see how much skill went into making them. The pottery was also special, with beautiful patterns painted on it. She loved looking at everything!
