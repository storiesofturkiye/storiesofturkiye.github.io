Karamel, the curious little cat, wanted to explore the hidden treasures of Turkey. She decided to visit beautiful and important places that not many people know about across the country. With excitement in her heart, she started a new adventure.

Amasya Karamel’s first stop was Amasya, a city famous for its lovely old houses from the Ottoman time and the rock tombs where the kings of Pontus were buried. She was amazed by how well nature and history mixed together.

Mardin.. Next, Karamel traveled to Mardin. It's a city known for its special buildings and beautiful sights! She walked around looking at the old stone houses and the tiny, winding streets.

Ahlat Karamel then visited Ahlat, a place famous for its old stone tombstones and lovely views of the lake. She learned about the ancient Seljuk cemetery and the long and interesting history of the area.

Sümela Monastery - Trabzon. In Trabzon, Karamel found the Sümela Monastery. It was built on top of a very high cliff! Karamel was so amazed by how beautiful the monastery looked and how old and important it was.

Göbekli Tepe.. Karamel's next adventure took her to Göbekli Tepe! It’s a very special place – the oldest temple ever found in the whole world. Karamel walked around the old ruins and discovered amazing things about how people lived a long, long time ago. She learned about the very first beginnings of human civilization.

Eğirdir Lake - Isparta Karamel then visited Eğirdir Lake, one of Türkiye's largest and most beautiful lakes. She enjoyed the peaceful scenery and the vibrant colors of the surrounding nature.

Safranbolu.. Finally, Karamel traveled to Safranbolu, a town that is famous for its old buildings from the Ottoman time. She walked around the lovely streets and looked at the historic buildings.
