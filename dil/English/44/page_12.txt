Central Anatolia Region – Wind Farm in Konya Karamel then traveled to Konya to visit a wind farm. She saw the giant wind turbines and learned how wind energy is harnessed to produce electricity. .
Konyalı Karamel went on an adventure! First, she went to a wind farm in Konya Karamel. Wow! She saw huge, tall wind turbines spinning around. They were so big! A friendly person showed her how these wind turbines make electricity. The wind pushes the big blades, and that makes the turbines turn. When they turn, they create power – electricity! It’s like magic, but it’s actually clever science using the wind. Konyalı Karamel was very impressed.
