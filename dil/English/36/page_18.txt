Southeastern Anatolia Region – Archaeological Discoveries at Göbekli Tepe.
Karamel’s last place to visit was Göbekli Tepe, a very old place where archaeologists are finding amazing things! She went there with the archaeologists and helped them discover secrets about the very first people who lived on Earth. They found old stones with pictures on them, and it tells us about how people lived a long, long time ago. It’s like a giant puzzle of the past that they are slowly putting together.
