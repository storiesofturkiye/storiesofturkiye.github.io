Karamel, the curious little cat, wanted to learn all about the wonderful stories from Türkiye. She decided to visit famous Turkish writers and poets, to find out about their books and the stories they made. With a happy feeling in her heart, she started a new adventure!

Hacı Bektaş Veli.. Nevşehir Karamel’s first stop was Nevşehir. There, she learned about Hacı Bektaş Veli. He was a very famous mystic and philosopher. She went to his museum and found out about his teachings and poems.

Karacaoğlan – Adana. Next, Karamel traveled to Adana to learn about Karacaoğlan. He was a famous poet who wrote beautiful and moving poems. . She went to a special place in Adana called a cultural center. There, she listened to folk songs. These songs were all about the stories Karacaoğlan told in his poems!

Bursa.. In Bursa, Karamel found the writings of Nazım Hikmet. Nazım Hikmet was a very important poet who wrote about things he believed were right and cared about people. She went to a library nearby and read some of his well-known poems.

Yaşar Kemal then went to Adana to learn about Yaşar Kemal. Yaşar Kemal is a very famous writer who writes stories about people who live in the countryside of Türkiye. She visited his house when he was a little boy and read some parts of his books.

Istanbul in Istanbul. Karamel also learned about Halide Edib Adıvar. Halide Edib was a very important writer and someone who loved her country, a nationalist. She went to Halide Edib's house museum. There, she looked at Halide Edib's books and learned about all the things she wrote and did.

Istanbul Karamel's next stop was to learn about Ahmet Hamdi Tanpınar, a very important writer of stories and poems. She went to his house museum and looked at all his books and writings.
