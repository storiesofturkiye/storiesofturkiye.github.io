Street Art - Kadıköy, Istanbul.
Finally, Karamel explored the street art of Kadıköy, Istanbul. She walked through the streets, amazed by the creativity and messages conveyed through murals and graffiti..
She saw colorful pictures painted on the walls. Some were big and showed animals, like playful cats and friendly dogs. Others were smaller and had words written on them. .
The street artists used spray paint to make these pictures. They created amazing designs with bright colors – reds, blues, yellows, and greens! Karamel looked closely at a mural with a smiling face. It made her feel happy..
She saw graffiti everywhere! Graffiti is like street art, but sometimes it's just someone's name or a short message. Some of the graffiti was funny, and some was thought-provoking. .
Karamel loved seeing all the different kinds of art. Each picture and word told a story. She learned that street art can make people smile, think, and even feel inspired! Kadıköy was full of amazing surprises for her to discover.
