Karamel's Adventure. Once upon a time, there was a curious little cat named Karamel who loved to explore nature. Karamel set off on a journey across Türkiye to discover the country's diverse plants and animals.
4
5
Marmara Region - Istanbul: Tulips and Bosphorus Dolphins. Karamel's first stop was in Istanbul. She saw so many beautiful tulips blooming everywhere! And guess what? She saw playful dolphins swimming in the Bosphorus.
6
7
Aegean Region - Izmir: Olive Trees and Caretta Caretta Turtles.. Next, Karamel traveled to Izmir. She explored the old olive groves there. She saw the Caretta Caretta turtles, which are in danger, on the sandy beaches.
8
9
Mediterranean Region - Antalya: Pine Forests and Loggerhead Turtles.. In Antalya, Karamel found lots of tall pine trees. He also saw loggerhead turtles laying their eggs on the beach!
10
11
Central Anatolia - Cappadocia: Fairy Chimneys and Rock Doves. In Cappadocia, Karamel was amazed by the special fairy chimneys and watched the rock doves flying around the old rock shapes.
12
13
Black Sea Region - Artvin: Tea Plantations and Brown Bears Karamel then traveled to Artvin. She explored the green tea plantations. She saw brown bears walking around in the thick forests.
14
15
Eastern Anatolia - Mount Ağrı: Snow Leopards and Edelweiss Flowers. In Eastern Anatolia, Karamel went to Mount Ağrı. She was very surprised and happy to see snow leopards and beautiful edelweiss flowers.
16
17
Southeastern Anatolia - Gobekli Tepe: Ancient Flora and Fauna. Karamel's last stop was Gobekli Tepe. She went to explore this very old place and learned about the plants and animals that lived there a long, long time ago. These plants and animals were there before people even started building houses!
18
19
Karamel was very happy and remembering all the wonderful things she had seen. She understood that the real treasures of Türkiye were all the different places in nature and the animals that lived there. She went back home, thinking about her next exciting adventure.
