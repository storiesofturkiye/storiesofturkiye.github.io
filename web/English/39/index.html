Karamel was a curious little cat who loved to explore and try new things. One day, she decided to go on a trip all across Türkiye to see how different places celebrated the four seasons. She was so excited that she packed her little backpack and started her adventure!
4
5
Spring in the Aegean Region.. Karamel’s first stop was the Aegean Region, where spring was bursting with life! She went to the lovely city of Izmir. There, she saw flowers of every color, trees covered in blossoms, and people having a wonderful time. They were celebrating the arrival of spring with music and dancing.
6
7
Summer in the Mediterranean Region.. Next, Karamel went to the Mediterranean Region. She experienced the hot and sunny summer in Antalya! She visited the beautiful beaches, swam in the clear water, and enjoyed the fun atmosphere of the summer festivals.
8
9
Karamel went to the Black Sea Region. She went to Rize and saw the beautiful fall leaves! The mountains were red, orange, and yellow. Karamel joined the people there and helped pick tea leaves. Picking tea leaves is something people do every autumn.
10
11
Winter in Eastern Anatolia.. Karamel’s next stop was the Eastern Anatolia Region, where she experienced the snowy winter in Erzurum. She enjoyed playing in the snow, watching people ski, and participating in traditional winter celebrations.
12
13
The Beauty of Spring in Cappadocia.. Karamel went to Cappadocia for the spring! She wanted to see the special springtime land. Karamel was amazed by all the pretty flowers that were blooming. The valleys were so green and bright! She looked at the amazing fairy chimneys and the colorful hot air balloons flying in the sky. It was a beautiful sight!
14
15
Summer Adventures in Southeastern Anatolia.. In the Southeastern Anatolia Region, Karamel was enjoying the warm summer weather. She went to see old and important places. She also enjoyed listening to traditional music and watching dances at local festivals. It made her feel the wonderful history and culture of the area.
16
17
Autumn in Central Anatolia.. Karamel continued her trip to Central Anatolia. She loved seeing the beautiful golden colors of autumn everywhere! She went to the old city of Hattusa. There, she learned about how people lived a long, long time ago. The pretty autumn leaves were all around her.
18
19
Karamel returned home with a happy heart and lots of memories of the four seasons. She remembered that every season in Türkiye was special, with its own beautiful things to see and traditions to celebrate. Because of this, her adventure was truly something she would never forget!
