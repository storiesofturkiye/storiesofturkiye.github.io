Karamel, a playful little cat, loved exploring all the different seasons. One day, she decided to travel all over Türkiye to see the beautiful and fun things about winter.
4
5
Marmara Region - Istanbul:. Karamel's first stop in Istanbul was a beautiful city covered in snow! She was amazed to see the Hagia Sophia, which was completely covered in white snow. The old building looked so grand and impressive with all the snow on top.
6
7
Aegean Region - Izmir: Coastal Snowfall. Next, Karamel traveled to Izmir, where she saw something very special – coastal snowfall! It was a rare treat to see snow on the beach. The sight of the white snow against the blue sea was magical and something Karamel would never forget.
8
9
Mediterranean Region - Antalya: Winter Citrus Harvest. In Antalya, Karamel found the winter citrus harvest! She loved the fresh, juicy oranges and lemons. They were a wonderful treat because the weather was nice and mild in winter.
10
11
Snowball Fight. Karamel traveled to Ankara in Central Anatolia. There, she joined some children for a fun snowball fight! The air was cold and fresh, and everyone was laughing and excited as they threw snowballs at each other.
12
13
Winter Tea Harvest in Trabzon. In Trabzon, Karamel helped with the winter tea harvest. The green tea leaves looked so pretty against the snowy mountains! It was a beautiful sight to see the green tea growing in the cold, snowy weather.
14
15
Southeastern Anatolia - Diyarbakır: Snowy Diyarbakır Walls. Karamel’s next stop was Diyarbakır, a city with very old walls. She went to explore these walls, and they were covered in snow! The historical site looked so peaceful and beautiful in the winter.
16
17
Skiing Adventure In Erzurum.. In Eastern Anatolia, in a city called Erzurum, Karamel had a wonderful time skiing! She slid down the snowy hills. It was so much fun! She loved the winter sports and the beautiful, snowy mountains all around her.
18
19
Winter Joy in Türkiye. Karamel was happy and excited about the winter. She loved trying new things! She realized that every part of Türkiye had its own special winter beauty. When she went home, she thought about her next exciting trip.
