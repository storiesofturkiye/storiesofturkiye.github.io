Karamel.. Once upon a time, in a sunny corner of the world called Türkiye, there lived a curious little cat named Karamel. Karamel loved adventures and discovering new treasures. One day, she found an old treasure map with different locations marked all over Türkiye. Excited, she set off on a journey to find these hidden treasures.

Marmara Region – Topkapı Palace Treasure. Karamel’s first stop was Istanbul, where she went to see the amazing Topkapı Palace. She found the palace's treasure room! It was full of sparkly jewels, shiny gold things, and old items from a long time ago. Each item told a story about the Ottoman Empire.

Aegean Region – The Wealth of Ephesus. Next, Karamel traveled to the ancient city of Ephesus. She walked around the old ruins and found the big library. It used to be full of scrolls and lots of knowledge. The ancient Greeks thought it was a real treasure!

Mediterranean Region – The Hidden Treasures of Aspendos. In the Mediterranean region, Karamel visited the ancient theatre of Aspendos. She discovered hidden vaults beneath the stage. These vaults held treasures from performances that happened a long, long time ago. Inside, she found ancient masks, beautiful costumes, and musical instruments!

Central Anatolia – The Gold of King Midas.. Karamel traveled to an old city called Gordium in Central Anatolia. This was the capital of the Phrygians a long time ago! She found the amazing treasure of King Midas. It had statues and things made of gold. Karamel learned a story about the king who could turn anything he touched into gold.

Black Sea Region – The Treasures of Sumela Monastery. In the Black Sea region, Karamel went to visit the Sumela Monastery. She climbed the steep cliffs, going up and up! And guess what she found? Hidden rooms! These rooms were filled with very old books, beautiful pictures of saints, and special things that were very valuable.

## The Treasure of Mount Ağrı. In Eastern Anatolia, a brave girl named Karamel went to Mount Ağrı. She had heard a story about Noah's Ark, the big boat that floated on the water long, long ago. . Karamel was curious and wanted to see if the story was true. While exploring the mountain, she found something amazing! It was hidden treasures. People believed these treasures were left behind from Noah's Ark. . She carefully looked at each item, wondering about the people who might have used them so many years ago. The treasures were a special reminder of a very old story and a great journey.

Southeastern Anatolia – The Gold of Göbekli Tepe. Karamel's last visit was to Southeastern Anatolia. There, she went to Göbekli Tepe, which is the oldest temple in the whole world! She found very old carvings made of stone. These carvings and some treasures buried there tell us stories about the very first people who lived a long, long time ago.
