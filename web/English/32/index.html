Karamel was a very curious little cat. She loved to explore new places! One day, Karamel decided to go on a trip across the Southeastern Anatolia Region in Türkiye. She wanted to learn all about the special culture, history, and beautiful nature of that area.

Gaziantep: Zeugma Mosaic Museum. Karamel’s first adventure was in a city called Gaziantep, and she went to the Zeugma Mosaic Museum. The museum had beautiful mosaics – that’s like pictures made of tiny colored stones! And there were also very old artworks to see. They were so amazing and interesting!
