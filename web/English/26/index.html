Karamel was a very curious little cat. She loved to explore new places! One day, Karamel decided to go on a trip across the Aegean Region in Türkiye. She wanted to learn all about the special things there – the history, the culture, and the beautiful nature.

Izmir.. Clock Tower Karamel’s first stop was Izmir. She looked at the famous Clock Tower in Konak Square. The Clock Tower was very beautiful, and the square was full of happy people. It made her feel happy to be there.

Izmir: Ancient City of Ephesus.. Next, Karamel went to Izmir. There, she explored the old city of Ephesus. She was very amazed by the big ruins and the long history of this ancient city. It was a very important city a long, long time ago!
