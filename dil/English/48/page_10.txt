Roman and Byzantine Art - Ephesus.
In Ephesus, Karamel discovered the amazing world of Roman and Byzantine art. She walked through the old city, looking at beautiful mosaics and shiny marble statues. .
The mosaics were like pictures made of tiny colored tiles. They told stories of brave heroes and happy times. The marble statues were so lifelike, they looked like real people! Karamel loved seeing all the beautiful art from so long ago. It showed how clever and creative the Roman and Byzantine people were. She spent the whole day exploring and admiring everything she saw. It was a wonderful adventure!
