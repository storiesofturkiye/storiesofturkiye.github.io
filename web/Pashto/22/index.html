Aegean Region - Izmir: Zayaw.. Next, Karamel went to Izmir for the olive harvest. She really liked picking olives and learning how they become yummy olive oil. . She would pick the olives from the trees. The olives were black and green. The people were smiling as they worked together. They put the olives in big baskets. Then, they took the baskets to a place where the olives were washed and crushed. . The crushed olives looked like a thick paste. They mixed the paste with water. After that, they made the olive oil! It smelled so good. Karamel learned that olive oil is very healthy and delicious to eat with bread. She was happy to help with the olive harvest in Izmir.
