Aegean Region - Izmir: Olive Harvest.
Next, Karamel traveled to Izmir, where she joined the olive harvest. She loved picking olives and learning about how they are turned into delicious olive oil. .
Karamel carefully walked through the olive trees. The trees were heavy with green and black olives! She gently touched the leaves and looked up at the branches full of fruit..
The people working in the olive grove used special tools to knock the olives off the branches. They gathered the olives in big baskets. Karamel helped too, picking up any olives that fell on the ground. .
She watched as the olives were taken to a special place. There, they were washed and crushed. The crushed olives turned into a thick, green liquid – olive oil! It smelled so wonderful..
Karamel learned that olive oil is used to make yummy food and it's very healthy for you. She was so happy to have helped with the olive harvest and learn about this special oil.
