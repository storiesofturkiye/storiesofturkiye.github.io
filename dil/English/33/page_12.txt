Central Anatolia - Whirling Dervishes in Konya.
Karamel went to Konya, a city in Central Anatolia. There, she saw something very special – the Whirling Dervishes! They were performing their spiritual dance..
The dervishes spun around and around, very gracefully. Karamel watched them, and she felt amazed and very peaceful inside. It was like watching a beautiful dream.
