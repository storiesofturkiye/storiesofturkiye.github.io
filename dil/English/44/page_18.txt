Water Conservation in Mardin.
Karamel went to Mardin in the southeastern part of Turkey to learn about ways people save water. She saw some amazing things!.
She saw how clever watering systems help farmers use less water when they grow plants. These systems are very efficient!.
Karamel also saw how people collect rainwater. When it rains, they gather the water in special places instead of letting it all run away. This helps them have more water to use later..
These ideas, like using efficient watering and collecting rainwater, are really helping people save water in Mardin, which is a dry place.
