## Mediterranean Region – The Lycian Civilization in Antalya. In Antalya, Karamel explored the ancient Lycian civilization. She learned about their special rock-cut tombs and the Lycian League. . The Lycians were a very interesting people who lived a long time ago in the area around Antalya. They were clever builders and had a unique way of burying their loved ones. Instead of building tombs underground, they carved them right into the cliffs! These rock-cut tombs are still there today, and they are very impressive.. The Lycians also formed something called the Lycian League. It was like a group of friends who all helped each other. They worked together to protect their lands and make their lives better. They were strong and brave, and they made their civilization very successful. Karamel was amazed by everything she learned about the Lycians – their tombs, their homes, and how they lived together as a league. It showed her how much people in the past were able to do!

Black Sea Region – The Kingdom of Pontus in Amasya. In Amasya, Karamel explored the remains of the Kingdom of Pontus. She learned about how important this old kingdom was to the people and history. . Karamel looked at old stones and walls. These were left behind by the people who lived in the Kingdom of Pontus a very long time ago. They were very clever people, and they built cities and homes. . She saw pictures carved into the stone. These pictures told stories about their lives, their gods, and their adventures. Karamel imagined what it was like to live in Pontus, with the sea all around and people who spoke a different language. . The Kingdom of Pontus was a special place. It was important for trade and for connecting different lands. Even though it's very old now, we can still learn a lot from it. Karamel felt amazed by the history she was discovering in Amasya.
