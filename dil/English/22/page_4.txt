Karamel was a curious little cat. She loved exploring all the different seasons. One day, Karamel decided to travel all across Türkiye. She wanted to see the beautiful things that happened in the fall..
The leaves on the trees were changing colors – red, yellow, and orange! Karamel watched them fall gently to the ground. She chased the leaves and played hide-and-seek among them. It was so much fun! .
She saw pumpkins everywhere! Big pumpkins and small pumpkins. Some were orange, some were white. Karamel even saw people carving faces into the pumpkins. .
The air smelled like cinnamon and apples. People were baking yummy treats. Karamel sniffed the air happily. She wished she could have a little taste!.
Karamel walked through fields of corn. The corn stalks were tall and dry. She saw farmers collecting the corn for the winter. It was a busy time of year. .
As the days got shorter, the sun set earlier. The sky turned a beautiful shade of purple and pink. Karamel curled up in a cozy spot, watching the stars come out. Fall was a magical time of year, and Karamel loved every minute of it.
