Sumerians and Göbekli Tepe.
Long ago, there were people called Sumerians. They lived a very long time ago in a place called Mesopotamia. The Sumerians built amazing things, like temples. These temples were some of the first big buildings ever made!.
Göbekli Tepe is a special place where the Sumerians built their temples. It's in a country called Turkey. People have dug up the temples there, and they are very old – even older than some of the biggest pyramids!.
The temples at Göbekli Tepe are covered in pictures. These pictures show animals like foxes, lions, and birds. The Sumerians were very good at carving these pictures into big stones. .
Why did the Sumerians build these temples? We're not sure exactly. But it seems like they were important places for ceremonies and gatherings. Maybe they worshipped gods or goddesses. .
Göbekli Tepe is a mystery! Scientists are still trying to learn more about the people who built it and why they built it. It shows us how clever and creative people were a very, very long time ago. These temples are like a window into the past, helping us understand what life was like for the Sumerians.
