Aegean Region – The Izmir Economic Congress (1923).. Next, Karamel went to Izmir. There, she learned about something very important that happened in 1923 called the Izmir Economic Congress. Central Anatolia – Ankara Becomes the Capital (1923). Karamel went to Ankara. There, she learned something very important! In 1923, Ankara was chosen to be the capital city of Türkiye. This means it became the most important city in the whole country. Karamel learned that this decision changed how Türkiye grew and developed.
