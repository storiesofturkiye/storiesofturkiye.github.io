Stuffed Grape Leaves.
Once upon a time, there was a very nice lady named Fatima. She loved to cook. One day, she decided to make stuffed grape leaves..
First, she took some soft grape leaves. Then, she mixed rice and yummy spices. She carefully put the rice and spices inside the grape leaves. .
She wrapped them up very neatly. Then, she cooked them in a pot. The smell was so good! Everyone loved Fatima's stuffed grape leaves. They were delicious and made everyone happy.
