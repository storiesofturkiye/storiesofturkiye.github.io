Karamel, the curious little cat, wanted to learn about the old stories and legends of Türkiye. She decided to visit different places to find these interesting stories and the magical creatures in them. With excitement in her heart, she started a new adventure.

The Legend of the Phoenix.. Hakkari Karamel’s first stop was Hakkari. There, she heard a story about the Phoenix, a magical bird. This bird is special because it can be born again from its own ashes! People say the Phoenix represents new beginnings and living forever.

The Story of the Simurgh - Mount Kaf. Next, Karamel traveled to the legendary Mount Kaf to learn about the Simurgh. The Simurgh is a huge bird that they say knows everything in the whole world! She listened carefully to stories about the Simurgh’s wisdom and how powerful it was.

9
The Tale of Shahmaran – Tarsus. In Tarsus, Karamel found a story about Shahmaran. Shahmaran is a magical creature. She is like a woman and a snake mixed together! . Karamel read that Shahmaran was very wise and beautiful. She also had a special secret: she could heal people! Her touch could make them feel better.

The Legend of Aspendos.. Antalya Karamel went to Antalya to hear a story about Aspendos. It was about a young architect who built a very special theater. He wanted to impress a princess and marry her! Antalya Karamel was amazed by how big and beautiful the old theater was.

13
The Myth of the Anatolian Dragon – Erzurum. In Erzurum, Karamel heard a story about the Anatolian Dragon. It was a very strong creature that lived in the mountains and protected treasures. She listened carefully as people told stories about brave heroes who were strong enough to face the dragon.

15
The Legend of the Lake Van Monster.. Van Karamel’s next stop was Van. She heard about the Lake Van Monster! People said it lived deep down in the lake. Van Karamel wanted to learn all about this mysterious creature. She explored the legends and stories that people told about the monster in the lake.

17
The Tale of the Golden Apple.. Finally, Karamel visited Kazdağları (Mount Ida) to learn about the tale of the Golden Apple. It’s a story about love and beauty that made the gods fight a big war! She found out about the magical beginnings of this famous story.

Karamel returned home with a heart full of amazement and her mind filled with old stories. She understood that the stories of Türkiye are full of wonderful tales and magical creatures.
