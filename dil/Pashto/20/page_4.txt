Once upon a time, there was a playful little cat named Karamel. One day, she decided to travel across Türkiye to learn about the delicious Turkish treats..
Karamel walked and walked. She saw green fields and tall mountains. She met many friendly dogs and birds along the way. .
In a small village, she found a baker making yummy bread. Karamel smelled the bread and her tummy rumbled! The baker gave her a tiny piece of bread, and Karamel gobbled it up happily..
Next, she came to a fruit stand. There were juicy apples, sweet grapes, and plump peaches. Karamel tried a little bit of each fruit. She loved the sweet taste!.
Karamel continued her journey. She saw a tea house where people were drinking hot tea with delicious cookies. The smell of the tea made Karamel feel warm and cozy..
Finally, Karamel returned home, tired but happy. She had learned so much about Türkiye's yummy treats! And she couldn't wait to tell all her friends about her adventure.
