Karamel the cat lived in a sunny place called Türkiye. Karamel loved two things very much: having adventures and eating yummy treats!.
One day, she found a colorful map. The map had a sparkly path that went through many different places in Türkiye. "Wow!" meowed Karamel. "This must be a treasure map!".
She decided right away to follow the map. She wanted to taste every special treat the map showed her where to find.
