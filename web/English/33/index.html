Karamel was a curious little cat who loved music and dancing. One sunny day, Karamel found a magical tambourine! This tambourine had a special power – it could take her to different places in Türkiye.. Karamel was very excited about this discovery. She decided to go on a musical journey to explore all the wonderful music and dances of Türkiye.

Marmara - Roman Music in Istanbul. Karamel’s first stop was Istanbul, and she heard the happy sounds of Roman music right away! She watched the musicians playing their bright and lively songs. It made her want to dance along with the beat.

Aegean - Zeybek Dance in İzmir. Next, Karamel traveled to İzmir, where she saw the amazing Zeybek dance. The dancers moved with so much grace and power! Karamel felt really inspired and wanted to try the dance moves herself.

9
Mediterranean - Yörük Music in Antalya. In Antalya, Karamel heard the happy sounds of Yörük music. The lively tunes and bright dances of the Yörük people made Karamel feel very happy inside.

11
Central Anatolia - Whirling Dervishes in Konya. Karamel went to Konya, a city in Central Anatolia. There, she saw something very special – the Whirling Dervishes! They were performing their spiritual dance.. The dervishes spun around and around, very gracefully. Karamel watched them, and she felt amazed and very peaceful inside. It was like watching a beautiful dream.

13
Black Sea - Horon Dance in Trabzon. In Trabzon, Karamel joined the lively Horon dance. The fast movements and happy music from the Black Sea area made Karamel’s paws excited to dance!
14
15
Eastern Anatolia - Kemençe Music in Erzurum. Karamel went on a trip to Erzurum. In Erzurum, she heard the beautiful sounds of a Kemençe. The Kemençe is a stringed instrument, and the music it made was very moving. It felt like a sad and happy story all at once! Karamel really loved the music. It helped her understand how old and special the traditions of this place are.

17
Southeastern Anatolia - Halay Dance in Diyarbakır.. Finally, Karamel arrived in Diyarbakır and joined the lively Halay dance! The dancers moved together in a rhythmic circle, and it made Karamel feel like she was part of a big, happy family.
