Yunus Emre in Eskişehir.
Karamel's journey went to Eskişehir. There, she learned about Yunus Emre. Yunus Emre was a very famous poet and a Sufi mystic. He wrote beautiful poems about love and being together. Karamel went to see Yunus Emre’s tomb. When she was there, she felt inspired by his words. His messages were all about love and how everyone is connected.
