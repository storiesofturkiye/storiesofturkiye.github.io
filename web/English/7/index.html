Once upon a time, there was a curious little cat named Karamel who loved to learn about history. Karamel set off on a journey across Türkiye to discover the key events and figures that shaped the nation's history.

Marmara Region - Istanbul: The Conquest of Istanbul Karamel's first stop was in Istanbul, where she learned about the Conquest of Istanbul. She imagined the great Fatih Sultan Mehmed II leading his troops to victory and transforming the city into a vibrant capital.

Mediterranean Region - Antalya: The Ancient City of Perge In Antalya, Karamel explored the ancient city of Perge. She walked through the grand gates and along the ancient streets, imagining the bustling life of this once-great city.

Eastern Anatolia - Erzurum: The Erzurum Congress In Erzurum, Karamel learned about the Erzurum Congress, a pivotal moment in the Turkish War of Independence. She imagined the passionate discussions and decisions that helped shape modern Türkiye.

Southeastern Anatolia - Göbekli Tepe: The World's Oldest Temple Karamel's final stop was Göbekli Tepe in Southeastern Anatolia. She explored the ancient site and learned about its significance as the world's oldest known temple, uncovering the mysteries of early civilization.
