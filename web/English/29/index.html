Karamel was a very curious little cat. She loved to go and see new places! One day, Karamel decided to travel through the Central Anatolia Region in Türkiye. She wanted to learn all about the special things there – the way people live, the stories from the past, and the beautiful nature.

Ankara: Anıtkabir Karamel's first stop was Ankara, where she visited Atatürk's Mausoleum. The big building and how important the place is made her feel really inspired.
