Karamel, a curious little cat, loved to explore and learn new things.. One day, Karamel found a map! The map showed different scientific discoveries made all over Türkiye. . Karamel was very excited. So, she started an adventure to find all these amazing discoveries on the map.

Marmara Region – Earthquake Research in the Marmara Sea. Karamel's first stop was the Marmara Sea. There, she met scientists who were studying earthquakes. They showed her the special machines they use to learn about these natural events and how they work to keep people safe.

Aegean Region – Ancient DNA Analysis in Izmir.. Next, Karamel went to Izmir. There, she met archaeologists. They were studying old DNA from things that were left behind a long time ago. Karamel learned that these scientists can find secrets from the past by looking at this DNA. It’s like they bring history to life!

Mediterranean Region – Marine Biology Research in Antalya. In Antalya, Karamel dove into the underwater world with marine biologists. She discovered the vibrant marine life and learned about the importance of protecting the ocean. . Karamel was so excited! She got to go diving in the sea near Antalya. She went with some people who study sea creatures – they are called marine biologists. . When Karamel swam underwater, she saw so many amazing things! Colorful fish swam past her. She saw corals that looked like plants and sea turtles gliding gracefully. There were all sorts of interesting animals living in the ocean. . The marine biologists showed Karamel how important it is to take care of the ocean. They explained that the ocean is home to many creatures, and we need to keep it clean and healthy so they can all live happily. Karamel learned that even small things, like not throwing trash into the sea, can help protect the ocean. She was happy to be part of helping the ocean!

Lake Tuz in Central Anatolia – Tiny Creatures in the Salt Lake. Karamel went to Lake Tuz. There, she met some scientists. They were studying tiny living things that live in a very salty place. Karamel was very surprised! She learned about these little creatures and how they are special and can live even in hard conditions.

Eastern Anatolia Region – Astronomy Discoveries in Erzurum. Karamel's next stop was Erzurum, where she visited an observatory. At the observatory, she met astronomers who were studying the stars and planets. She looked through a big telescope and learned about all the amazing things in space.

Southeastern Anatolia Region – Archaeological Discoveries at Göbekli Tepe. Karamel’s last place to visit was Göbekli Tepe, a very old place where archaeologists are finding amazing things! She went there with the archaeologists and helped them discover secrets about the very first people who lived on Earth. They found old stones with pictures on them, and it tells us about how people lived a long, long time ago. It’s like a giant puzzle of the past that they are slowly putting together.
