Once upon a time, there was a curious 
little cat named Karamel who loved 
music and dancing. One sunny day, 
Karamel found a magical tambourine 
that could transport her to different 
parts of Türkiye. Excited by the discovery, 
Karamel decided to embark on 
a musical journey to explore 
the rich musical traditions
 and dances of  Türkiye.
