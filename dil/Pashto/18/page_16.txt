Sumerians and Göbekli Tepe ..
Çú Sumerians and Göbekli Tepe were very old people who lived a long, long time ago. They built amazing places called temples. These temples are very special and interesting to see..
The Sumerians lived in a land far away, and they were very clever. They knew a lot about stars and numbers. They built their temples with big stones. The stones were very heavy, but the Sumerians were strong. They wanted to build these temples for their gods..
Göbekli Tepe is a special place where the Sumerians built some of their temples. It's like a big, old city made of stones. The stones have pictures on them – pictures of animals like foxes, lions, and birds. These pictures are very old, and they tell us about what the Sumerians believed..
The temples at Göbekli Tepe are very mysterious. We don't know exactly why the Sumerians built them, but we know they were very important to them. They spent a lot of time and effort building these temples. They wanted to have a place to pray to their gods and to celebrate special events..
The temples built by the Sumerians are some of the oldest temples in the whole world. They show us how people in the past were creative and strong. We can still see these temples today, and they remind us of the amazing things that people can do.
