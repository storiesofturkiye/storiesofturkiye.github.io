Hacı Bektaş Veli in Nevşehir.
Karamel's adventure took her to Nevşehir, a special place. There, she learned about Hacı Bektaş Veli, who was a very wise thinker and a spiritual leader. She went to the Hacı Bektaş Veli Museum. At the museum, she discovered all about his important ideas. He taught people about being equal and treating everyone with kindness – that's called humanism.
