Once upon a time, there was a curious little cat named Karamel, who loved to explore and celebrate. Karamel set off on a journey across Turkey to discover the unique holidays and festivals in each region.

Istanbul – Marmara Region: Republic Day Karamel’s first stop was Istanbul, where she joined the Republic Day celebrations. The streets were filled with people waving flags, singing songs, and watching fireworks.
