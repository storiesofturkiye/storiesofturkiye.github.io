Fall Festivals in Erzurum.
In Eastern Anatolia, in a city called Erzurum, Karamel was so happy! It was fall, and there were lots of fun festivals happening. .
Karamel loved listening to traditional music. The music made her want to move and dance! She watched people dancing in colorful clothes. Everyone was smiling and having a wonderful time celebrating the fall season. It was a very festive and joyful day for Karamel.
