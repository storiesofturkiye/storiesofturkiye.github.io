Karamel, a little cat, was very curious. She loved to explore and see new things in nature. One day, Karamel decided she wanted to see all the different places in Türkiye and the animals that lived there.. She packed a small bag with her favorite treats and set off on her adventure. First, she walked through green forests with tall trees. Birds sang sweetly from the branches above. . Next, Karamel came to a sparkling blue lake. Fish jumped out of the water, and ducks swam gracefully by. She watched them for a long time, fascinated.. After that, she climbed up a rocky mountain. The view from the top was amazing! She could see the whole valley below, with fields of flowers and little villages. . Karamel saw playful squirrels chasing each other, colorful butterflies fluttering around, and even a friendly deer grazing in a meadow. She made new friends with a little field mouse who showed her secret paths.. As the sun began to set, Karamel started to walk back home. She was tired but happy. She had seen so many wonderful things in Türkiye! It was the best adventure ever.

Central Anatolia - Cappadocia: Fairy Chimneys and Birds. Karamel went to Cappadocia! She was very happy to see the amazing fairy chimneys. These are tall, pointy rocks that look like chimneys! She also saw lots of birds building their homes in the rocks. It was so interesting to watch them.

Uzungöl and Waterfowl in Trabzon.. In Trabzon, Karamel went to Uzungöl. Uzungöl is a very peaceful lake. It has green forests all around it. Karamel watched the ducks and geese swimming on the lake. They moved so gently across the water!

Eastern Anatolia - Mount Ağrı: Snow Leopards and Eagles. In Eastern Anatolia, Karamel went exploring the snowy mountains of Mount Ağrı. He saw some amazing animals! He spotted snow leopards, which are like big, fluffy cats that live in the mountains. And he also saw majestic eagles soaring high in the sky. They were very beautiful to watch!

Southeastern Anatolia - Göbekli Tepe: Ancient Ruins and Wildlife. Karamel’s last stop was Göbekli Tepe. There, she found amazing old ruins! She also watched the animals that lived in that place. . Göbekli Tepe was a very special place from a long, long time ago. People who lived there built giant stone circles. These circles had pictures of animals like foxes, lions, and birds. No one knows exactly why they built them, but they were very important to the people of Göbekli Tepe.. Karamel looked closely at the stones. Some of them were very big! She saw birds carved into the stone. They looked like they were flying. There were also animals with big teeth, like lions and foxes. It was like looking at a story from the past. . While she was there, Karamel saw all sorts of wildlife. She saw deer walking in the grass. Birds were singing in the trees. Sometimes, she even saw wild dogs running around! The animals seemed to be very comfortable living near the ancient ruins. . Göbekli Tepe showed Karamel how people long ago lived and what they believed in. It was a wonderful place to learn about history and see the animals that share this special land.
