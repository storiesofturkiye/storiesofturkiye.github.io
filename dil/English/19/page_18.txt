Eastern Anatolia - Ardahan: Birdirbir.
While exploring Eastern Anatolia, Karamel found a group of children playing a fun game called Birdirbir. She was curious and wanted to play too, so she joined them. .
The game was simple! They had to jump over a child who was crouching down, and every time they jumped, they shouted "Birdirbir!" .
Karamel loved to play and was very quick and good at jumping. She became the best player in the game, and the children were so happy to have her play with them.
