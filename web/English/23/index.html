Karamel, a little cat, was very curious. She loved to explore all the different seasons. One day, Karamel decided to travel all over Türkiye to see how beautiful and fun summer was.
4
5
Marmara Region - Istanbul: Bosphorus Cruise. Karamel’s first stop was Istanbul! She went on a boat trip along the Bosphorus. The water sparkled, and the cool wind felt nice. It made the city look even more beautiful and magical.
6
7
Aegean Region - Izmir: Beach Fun Next. Karamel traveled to Izmir, where she played on the sandy beaches. The clear blue water and warm sun made it a perfect day for beach fun. . She built a big sandcastle with moats and towers. The waves came and tickled the sandcastle, but Karamel kept building higher! She splashed in the water and collected shiny seashells. . A little crab scuttled past her feet, and she giggled. She spent the whole day laughing and playing in the sand and sea. It was a wonderful beach day in Izmir!
8
9
Mediterranean Region - Antalya: Water Sports In Antalya. In Antalya, Karamel found something really exciting: water sports! She tried lots of fun things, like riding a jet ski – it was super fast! And she went parasailing, which means she floated up in the air with a big parachute. It felt amazing to see the beautiful coastline from way up high. She loved the rush of excitement and the lovely sea all around.
10
11
Central Anatolia - Cappadocia: Hot Air Balloon Ride. Karamel went to Cappadocia for a hot air balloon ride! From up high in the balloon, the amazing view of the fairy chimneys and the rocks was even more beautiful than she imagined.
12
13
Black Sea Region - Rize: Tea Gardens. In Rize, Karamel visited the beautiful tea gardens. The green hills and fresh air made it a peaceful and happy place on her summer trip. . The tea plants grew everywhere, all neat and tidy. Karamel walked along the paths, looking at the rows and rows of green leaves. It smelled so fresh and sweet! She took a deep breath of the clean air and smiled. The tea gardens in Rize were a wonderful stop for her summer journey.
14
15
Southeastern Anatolia - Gaziantep: Ice Cream Treat. Karamel's next stop was Gaziantep, where she enjoyed a delicious ice cream treat. The Turkish ice cream was very special! It had amazing flavors and different textures that made it a delightful experience.
16
17
Lake Van, Eastern Anatolia - Van.. In Van, Karamel really liked looking at Lake Van. The water in Lake Van was so clear, like crystal! And the mountains behind it were beautiful too. It was a perfect place to relax and feel happy.

19
Karamel was so happy, her heart felt like it was full of summer! She had seen so many new things in Türkiye, and she realized that every part of the country has its own special summer feeling. When she went home, she kept thinking about her next exciting adventure in a different season.
