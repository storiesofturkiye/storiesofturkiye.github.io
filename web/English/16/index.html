Once upon a time, in a land full of wonders called Turkey, lived a curious cat named Karamel. Karamel loved exploring new places and learning about different arts and crafts. One sunny morning, she found a colorful map that led her to various cities known for their unique artistic traditions.

Calligraphy in Istanbul Karamel’s first stop was Istanbul, where she visited a calligraphy workshop. She watched a master calligrapher creating beautiful Arabic calligraphy, with graceful lines and elegant curves that told stories on paper.
