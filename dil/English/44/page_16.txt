Erzurum, Eastern Anatolia Region – Geothermal Energy.
Karamel’s journey took her to Erzurum! She went to see a special place called a geothermal energy plant. .
At the plant, she learned something amazing. The Earth is very warm inside! This heat is used to make clean energy for everyone to use. It's like the Earth is giving us a warm hug that we can turn into power! .
The plant takes the heat from deep underground and turns it into electricity. This electricity can then be used to light up homes, schools, and hospitals – all without making a mess or polluting the air. It’s a really clever way to get energy!
