Karamel, a curious little cat, loved to explore and have fun! One day, Karamel started a journey all across Türkiye. She wanted to find out about all the special holidays and celebrations in every part of the country. . She went to different cities and towns. In each place, she saw people celebrating in amazing ways. She learned about games they played, foods they ate, and songs they sang. Karamel had a wonderful time discovering all the different festivals and holidays Türkiye had to offer. It was a very exciting adventure for a curious little cat!

Erzurum Winter Festival. In Erzurum, which is in Eastern Anatolia, Karamel went to the Winter Festival! Everyone was having so much fun in the snowy mountains. They were ice skating, skiing, and playing with snowballs. It was a very happy day for everyone.

Southeastern Anatolia - Gaziantep: Pistachio Festival. Karamel's last stop was Gaziantep for the Pistachio Festival! The whole city was happy and busy.
