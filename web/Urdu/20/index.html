Stuffed Grape Leaves. یہation, rice aur spices se bharay hue hain. (یہation, rice اور spices سے بھرا ہوا ہے۔). They are a very popular dish in many countries. People make them with love and care. It takes time to make them, but they taste very delicious!. The leaves are usually grape leaves, but sometimes other leaves are used too. First, the leaves are softened by steaming or boiling them. Then, a mixture of rice, spices like cumin, coriander, and sometimes mint, is prepared. . A spoonful of this mixture is placed on the leaf and then it is carefully rolled up. These rolled-up leaves are then cooked in water or a sauce. . Stuffed grape leaves are often eaten as a side dish with yogurt or a salad. They are a healthy and tasty part of many cultures! Everyone loves to eat them, especially when they are made with lots of love.
