Eastern Anatolia - Erzurum: Evliya Çelebi was a famous traveler and writer. He traveled all over and wrote about the places he saw. .
Evliya Çelebi was very curious. He wanted to see everything in the world! So, he went on many long trips. He traveled through mountains and valleys, across deserts and plains. .
Everywhere he went, Evliya Çelebi carefully watched and wrote down what he saw. He wrote about the cities, the people, the buildings, and the customs. He wrote about the beautiful landscapes and the interesting things he learned..
His writings are like a treasure chest of stories from the past. They help us understand what the world was like a long, long time ago. Evliya Çelebi's stories are still exciting to read today!
