Trabzon, Karamel da Winter Tea Harvest ma help ka.. Trabzon ma winter ma tea leaves ka very beautiful look. Green tea leaves snow ma very nice look. . Winter ma tea harvest ka time, Karamel very hard work ka. She pick the tea leaves carefully. The snow ma green tea leaves very beautiful look.. Karamel and other people work together. The winter tea harvest in Trabzon is very important. It gives people jobs and makes tea for everyone to enjoy. The green tea leaves against the snowy mountains look very beautiful.
