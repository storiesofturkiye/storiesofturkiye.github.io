Eastern Anatolia - Ardahan: 
Birdirbir
While exploring Eastern Anatolia, 
Karamel came across a group of children 
playing a traditional game called Birdirbir. 
Curious, she decided to join them. 
The game involved jumping over a crouched child 
and chanting "Birdirbir!" with each leap. 
Karamel's playful spirit and agility 
made her a star of the game, 
much to the delight of the children.
