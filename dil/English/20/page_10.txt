Mediterranean Region - Antalya: Fruit Kebabs..
The colorful skewers of fresh fruits were both healthy and delicious. They were a special treat enjoyed in Antalya. People carefully chose the ripest fruits, like juicy watermelon, sweet grapes, and soft figs. They threaded the fruit onto wooden sticks, making little fruit kebabs. .
These weren't just yummy snacks; they were also good for you! Eating fresh fruit helps you grow big and strong. The sweetness of the fruit was perfect, especially on a warm day. Everyone loved to eat these colorful, healthy treats in Antalya. They were a delightful way to enjoy the sunshine and the delicious fruits of the region.
