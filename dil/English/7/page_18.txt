Southeastern Anatolia - Göbekli Tepe: The World's Oldest Temple.
Karamel’s last stop was Göbekli Tepe in Southeastern Anatolia. She went to see the old place and learned why it is so important. Göbekli Tepe is the world's oldest temple that people have found! Karamel explored the ruins and tried to understand the secrets of the very first people who built it. It helps us learn about how people lived a long, long time ago.
