Karamel the little cat was very curious. She loved to explore new places and learn about different things. One day, Karamel decided she wanted to learn about Türkiye! She wanted to find out what people in Türkiye wear – their special clothes and how they dress. .
She started by visiting a library. The librarian showed her beautiful pictures of women wearing colorful headscarves and flowing dresses. There were men wearing vests and sometimes fezzes, which are like soft, round hats..
Karamel saw pictures of embroidered jackets with shiny beads and threads. Some clothes were made of soft cotton, perfect for the warm weather. Others were made of wool, to keep people warm in the cooler months. .
She learned that different parts of Türkiye had their own special styles of clothing. The colors were bright and cheerful – red, blue, green, and yellow! Sometimes, they used patterns with flowers and stars..
Karamel was amazed by all the beautiful clothes. She thought it was wonderful how each piece of clothing told a story about the people who wore them and where they came from. She decided she wanted to learn even more about Türkiye's clothes!
