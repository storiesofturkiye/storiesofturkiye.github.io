Karamel, the adventurous little cat, wanted to learn about eco-friendly practices and sustainable living in Turkey. With excitement in her heart, she set off on a journey to explore various eco-friendly initiatives across the country.
4

Marmara Region – An organic farm in Bursa Karamel’s first stop was an organic farm in Bursa. She learned about organic farming practices, helped plant vegetables, and enjoyed fresh, healthy produce.

7

Mediterranean Region – Sea Turtle Conservation in Dalyan In Dalyan, Karamel joined a sea turtle conservation project. She helped protect the nesting sites of the endangered Caretta caretta turtles and learned about marine conservation.
