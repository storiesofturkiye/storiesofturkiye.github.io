Once upon a time, there was a curious little cat named Karamel who loved to explore and discover. One day, she decided to dive into the rich maritime history of Türkiye, eager to learn about ancient ports, famous ships, and the stories of sailors..
Karamel was very excited! She wanted to know how people used to travel by sea a long, long time ago. She read many books and looked at pictures..
She learned about old cities near the sea where boats would dock. These were called ports. The ships in these ports were very special. Some were big and strong, and some were fast and sleek..
Karamel read stories about brave sailors who sailed far away on these ships. They faced big waves and stormy weather, but they never gave up. They told stories of faraway lands and amazing creatures..
She learned about the history of Türkiye’s seas and how important ships were for trade and for connecting people from different places. Karamel found it all very interesting! She dreamed of sailing on one of those grand ships herself someday.
