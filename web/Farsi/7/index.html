Once upon a time, there was a curious little cat named Karamel who loved to learn about history. Karamel started a journey across Türkiye to find out about the important things and people that made Türkiye the country it is today. . Karamel saw old buildings and talked to people who remembered stories from long ago. He learned about brave warriors and wise leaders. He visited places where important events happened, like ancient castles and historical cities. . Karamel listened carefully to all the stories he heard. He learned how people lived a long time ago, what they liked, and what they were proud of. He discovered that Türkiye has a very rich and interesting history filled with many exciting stories. Karamel was so happy to learn all about his country’s past!
