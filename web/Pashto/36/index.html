Once upon a time, there was a curious little cat named Karamel who loved to explore and learn new things.. One day, Karamel found a map showing different scientific discoveries made across Türkiye. Excited, Karamel set off on an adventure to uncover these amazing discoveries.. Karamel looked at the map with big eyes. The map showed pictures of things people in Türkiye had discovered – like how to make things fly and how to talk to each other far away!. Karamel decided to visit each place on the map. First, she went to a place where they learned about the stars. She saw big telescopes and learned that the stars are very, very far away. . Next, Karamel traveled to a place where people discovered how to build tall buildings. She looked up at the skyscrapers and was amazed! They were so high!. Then, Karamel went to a place where they figured out how to send messages using radio waves. She listened to the radio and heard music and stories from faraway places. It was very interesting!. Karamel visited many different places on the map. She learned so much about what people in Türkiye had discovered. She was happy to have gone on her adventure and learned all about science.
