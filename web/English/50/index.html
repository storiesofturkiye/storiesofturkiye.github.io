Karamel, the adventurous cat, heard stories about the mystical caves of Türkiye. Eager to discover their secrets, she set off on a journey to explore these ancient wonders.
4
5
The Caves of Cappadocia.. Karamel’s first stop was in Cappadocia! She went to see the amazing fairy chimneys and the old cave homes where people used to live. Karamel was very impressed by the special rocks shaped like chimneys and all the stories hidden in the stone.

7
The Altınbeşik Cave.. Next, Karamel went to see the Altınbeşik Cave in Antalya. She was very surprised to see the lake inside the ground and the beautiful rocks made of limestone. The cave was so beautiful that Karamel couldn't say anything!
8
9
The Karain Cave Karamel went to the Karain Cave. It’s a very old cave where people used to live in Türkiye! She found old tools and things made by the first people who lived there long, long ago. By looking at them, she learned how important the Karain Cave is in history.
10
11
The Dim Cave in Alanya.. Karamel explored the Dim Cave. She was very amazed by the stalactites and stalagmites that decorated the cave. The cool and mysterious air made her feel like she was in a different world.
12
13
İnsuyu Cave Karamel’s next stop was the İnsuyu Cave in Burdur. She looked with wonder at the colorful rocks and shiny things inside. There were also small lakes hidden underground! The cave was so beautiful; it really took her breath away.
14
15
Dupnisa Cave in Kırklareli.. Karamel visited the Dupnisa Cave. She learned that the cave has two parts: a wet cave and a dry cave.. She explored both the wet and the dry parts of the cave. While exploring, she found amazing rock shapes and groups of bats living there.
16
17
Yedi Göller Cave Karamel's final stop was the Yedi Göller Cave in Kastamonu. She was amazed by the seven little lakes inside the cave, each one special in its own way. The peacefulness of the cave made it a wonderful ending to her trip.
18
19
Karamel was full of amazement and thought of many stories in her head. She understood that the real treasures of Türkiye were not just what you could see above ground, but something special hidden underneath. Then she went back home, thinking about her next exciting adventure.
