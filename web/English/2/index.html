Once upon a time, there was a curious little cat named Karamel who loved music. Karamel set off on a journey across Türkiye to discover the unique musical instruments of each region.
4
5
Marmara Region - Istanbul: Ney and Ottoman Culture Karamel's first stop was Istanbul, where she met an old man playing the ney. The soft, soothing sound of the ney filled the air. She also saw the majestic Topkapi Palace and learned about the rich Ottoman culture.
6
7
Aegean Region - Izmir: Zurna and Traditional Ege Dance Next, Karamel traveled to Izmir in the Aegean region. Here, she heard the lively sound of the zurna and watched people perform the traditional Ege dance. The zurna's cheerful tunes made everyone dance joyfully.
8
9
Mediterranean Region - Antalya: Ud and Seaside Culture Karamel's journey continued to Antalya, where she met a woman playing the ud by the seaside. The beautiful melodies of the ud reflected the tranquility of the Mediterranean coast. She also enjoyed delicious seafood.
10
11
Central Anatolia - Ankara: Baglama and Anatolian Folklore In Central Anatolia, Karamel visited Ankara. She encountered a group of young people playing the baglama and singing traditional Anatolian folk songs. The baglama's soulful sound told stories of the Anatolian plains.
12
13
Black Sea Region - Trabzon: Kemençe and Horon Dance Karamel then traveled to Trabzon in the Black Sea region. She heard the fast-paced sound of the kemençe and saw people performing the energetic horon dance. The kemençe's lively tunes matched the rhythm of the waves.
14
15
Eastern Anatolia - Erzurum: Tulum and Mountain Traditions In Eastern Anatolia, Karamel visited Erzurum and met a shepherd playing the tulum. The tulum's powerful sound echoed through the mountains. She also learned about the region's rich traditions and tasted local delicacies.
16
17
Southeastern Anatolia - Gaziantep: Davul and Zurna, and Culinary Delights Karamel's final stop was Gaziantep in Southeastern Anatolia. She joined a festive celebration with the rhythmic beats of the davul and zurna. She also savored delicious baklava and other local treats.

19
With her heart full of music and her belly full of tasty treats, Karamel realized that Türkiye's true treasures were its rich culture and the wonderful people she met along the way. She returned home, dreaming of her next adventure.
