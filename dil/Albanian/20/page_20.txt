She returned home, tired from her long journey. The sun was setting, painting the sky with beautiful colors of orange and pink. She walked through the garden, where flowers of all colors were blooming. The air smelled sweet and fresh. .
She opened the door to her house and stepped inside. It was warm and cozy. Her mother was waiting for her in the kitchen, smiling. “Welcome home, my dear,” she said. “I was worried about you.”.
The girl smiled back. “I had a long day,” she said. “But it was worth it.” She told her mother all about her adventures. Her mother listened carefully, and when she was finished, she hugged her tight. .
“I’m so glad you’re home safe,” her mother said. “Now, let’s have some dinner.” They ate dinner together, talking and laughing. The girl felt happy and loved. She knew that there was no place like home.
