The temples at Göbekli Tepe are very mysterious. We don't know exactly why the Sumerians built them, but we know they were very important to them. They spent a lot of time and effort building these temples. They wanted to have a place to pray to their gods and to celebrate special events.. The temples built by the Sumerians are some of the oldest temples in the whole world. They show us how people in the past were creative and strong. We can still see these temples today, and they remind us of the amazing things that people can do.
