Karamel, the curious little cat, wanted to learn about the different families in Türkiye. Türkiye has seven different regions, and each region has its own special family traditions! With excitement in her heart and a map in her paw, she started a new adventure to visit families in each region. . She wanted to see how they lived, what they ate, and what stories they told. Karamel was very eager to learn about everyone. Her little paws carried her across mountains and through valleys, towards the first family she planned to meet. She couldn't wait to make new friends!

Marmara Region - Istanbul Family Karamel's first stop was in Istanbul. She visited a family who lived in a busy part of the city. She saw how quickly people moved around and had a warm dinner with the family. They looked out at the beautiful Bosphorus.
