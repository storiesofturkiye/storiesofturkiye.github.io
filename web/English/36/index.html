Once upon a time, there was a curious little cat named Karamel who loved to explore and learn new things. One day, Karamel found a map showing different scientific discoveries made across Turkey. Excited, Karamel set off on an adventure to uncover these amazing discoveries.
4
5
Marmara Region – Earthquake Research in the Marmara Sea Karamel’s first stop was the Marmara Sea, where she met scientists studying earthquakes. They showed her the special technology they use to understand these natural events and how they work to keep people safe.

7
Aegean Region – Ancient DNA Analysis in Izmir Next, Karamel traveled to Izmir, where she met archaeologists analyzing ancient DNA from historical remains. She learned how these scientists uncover secrets of the past and bring history to life.

Mediterranean Region – Marine Biology Research in Antalya In Antalya, a Karamel dove discovered the vibrant marine life and learned about the importance of protecting the ocean.

Central Anatolia – Microbial Life in Lake Tuz Karamel then visited Lake Tuz, where she met scientists studying microorganisms that live in extreme conditions. She was amazed to learn about these tiny creatures and their unique adaptations.

13
Black Sea Region – Climate Change Research in Rize In Rize, Karamel joined researchers studying the effects of climate change on the local environment. She saw how they collected data and worked to understand and protect the natural world.
14
15
Eastern Anatolia Region – Astronomy Discoveries in Erzurum Karamel’s next stop was Erzurum, where she visited an observatory and met astronomers studying the stars and planets. She gazed through a telescope and learned about the wonders of the universe.

17
Southeastern Anatolia Region – Archaeological Discoveries at Göbekli Tepe Karamel’s final stop was Göbekli Tepe, an ancient archaeological site. She explored the area with archaeologists and uncovered secrets of early human civilization.

With her heart full of wonder and her mind filled with amazing discoveries, Karamel returned home, dreaming of her next adventure. She realized that the true treasure of Turkey was its rich history and the incredible work of its scientists.
