Karamel, a curious cat, lived in a wonderful land called Türkiye. Karamel loved to explore and learn about different things like painting and making things with her paws!. One sunny morning, Karamel found a colorful map. The map showed the way to many cities. Each city was famous for its special art.

Calligraphy in Istanbul.. Karamel’s first stop was Istanbul, where she went to a calligraphy workshop. She watched a master calligrapher making beautiful Arabic calligraphy. The lines were graceful, and the curves were elegant. They told stories on the paper.

Ceramics in Kütahya.. Next, Karamel went to Kütahya. Kütahya is a city that's very famous for making beautiful ceramics! She watched artists who were very skilled at painting amazing designs on tiles and pottery. The bright colors and the tiny details of the patterns really amazed Karamel.

Carpet Weaving in Konya. Karamel's journey went on to Konya, and she visited a place where they made carpets! She was very impressed by how skillful and patient the people were. They carefully wove beautiful designs and bright colors into every single carpet.

Shadow Puppetry in Bursa. In Bursa, Karamel found a magical world of shadow puppets. She watched a very skilled puppeteer make characters like Karagöz and Hacivat come alive! These characters told funny stories that also taught people things.

Jewelry Making in Mardin.. Karamel went to a city called Mardin. In Mardin, she met people who were very good at making jewelry. They made beautiful things to wear! Karamel loved watching them work. The jewelry was so detailed, and the gems sparkled like stars.

Glass Art in Istanbul. Karamel went back to Istanbul for an adventure! She visited a special place where people made beautiful things out of glass. The artists were like magic makers. They carefully shaped the hot glass and added colors to it. It was amazing to watch them create stunning works of art.

17
Leatherwork in Gaziantep. Karamel’s journey took her to Gaziantep, where she discovered something amazing: leatherwork! She watched people making beautiful things from leather. They carefully shaped and stitched the leather to create designs. These weren't just pretty; they were also useful! The artisans made wonderful items like bags, belts, and shoes.

Ebru Art in Bursa.. Finally, Karamel went to Bursa to see something amazing called ebru, or water marbling. She watched artists carefully drop colors onto water. The colors made beautiful, swirling patterns. Then, they gently picked up the paper and the patterns stayed there! It was like magic.
