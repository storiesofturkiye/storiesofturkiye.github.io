Karamel.. Once upon a time, in a sunny corner of the world called Türkiye, there lived a curious little cat named Karamel. Karamel loved adventures and animals. One day, she found a colorful map with sparkling paths that led to Türkiye's unique animals and their homes. Excited, Karamel set off to meet these wonderful creatures.

Marmara Region - Dolphins in the Bosphorus, Istanbul. Karamel's first stop was Istanbul, where she sailed on the Bosphorus Strait. She saw playful dolphins jumping in and out of the water! The dolphins were showing off amazing tricks and racing next to the boats.

Aegean Region - Olive Ridley Sea Turtles in Muğla Story.. Next, Karamel traveled to the beautiful beaches of Muğla, where she met olive ridley sea turtles. These gentle creatures were busy laying their eggs in the warm sand, preparing for the arrival of their tiny hatchlings.

Mediterranean Region - Caretta Caretta Turtles in Antalya.. In Antalya, Karamel met the amazing caretta caretta turtles. She watched with wonder as these big turtles swam beautifully in the clear water and laid their eggs on the sandy beach.

Wild Horses in Cappadocia. In the heart of Türkiye, Karamel went to see the beautiful place called Cappadocia. There, she saw wild horses running freely all over the big, rocky land. Their hair, called manes, flowed in the wind as they ran.

Black Sea Region – Migratory Birds in the Kızılırmak Delta.. Karamel traveled to the Kızılırmak Delta in the Black Sea region. She was very surprised to see so many birds! Lots of different kinds of birds stopped there to rest and eat. It was a very busy and happy place with all the birds around.

Eastern Anatolia – Van Cats in Van:. In Eastern Anatolia, Karamel went to Van and met the famous Van cats. These beautiful cats had different colored eyes – one blue and one yellow! They were very friendly and loved to play. Karamel was happy watching them do all sorts of fun things.

Southeastern Anatolia – Gazelles in Şanlıurfa. Karamel's last stop was Şanlıurfa, and there she saw beautiful gazelles. She watched them move gracefully across the wide plains. Their thin bodies and quick steps made Karamel feel amazed.

Karamel felt amazed and had lots of stories in her head. She understood that the real treasures of Türkiye were its wonderful animals and the places they lived. She went home happy because of all the different animals and beautiful sights she saw. She was already thinking about her next exciting trip!
