Karamel.. Once upon a time, there was a curious little cat named Karamel who loved to explore and discover. One day, she decided to visit traditional Turkish villages to experience the simple and beautiful village life and learn about local customs and traditions.

Village Welcome.. Karamel's first stop was a charming village hidden among the hills. The people in the village were very friendly and happy to see her. They welcomed her warmly and invited her to do things with them every day, like their usual activities and celebrations.

Harvesting in the Fields.. Karamel joined the villagers in the fields, where they were harvesting crops. She learned how to pick fruits and vegetables and enjoyed the fresh, earthy scents of the countryside.

Making Bread Karamel helped in making traditional bread. The villagers showed her how to knead the dough and bake it in a stone oven. The smell of freshly baked bread filled the air.

Folk Dance and Music.. In the evening, Karamel saw a lively folk dance! The villagers played traditional instruments, like drums and flutes. They danced with so much joy, and they showed Karamel some of their dance moves. Karamel tried her best to copy them, and she had a lot of fun! It was a wonderful evening of music and dancing.

Local Crafts Karamel went to see the people who made beautiful things with their hands. She watched them make pottery – round bowls and pots! They wove carpets with colorful threads, making pretty patterns. And they created jewelry – necklaces, bracelets, and rings – that were very detailed and lovely. Karamel was so amazed by how skilled they were!

Village Feast.. The villagers prepared a grand feast with traditional dishes. Karamel enjoyed the delicious food and felt happy to be with everyone celebrating together.

Storytelling by the Fire.. As the night grew darker, everyone gathered around a bonfire. The elders told stories of the village’s past, and Karamel listened intently, enchanted by the tales.
