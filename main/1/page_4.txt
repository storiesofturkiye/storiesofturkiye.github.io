Once upon a time, in a sunny corner
of  the world called Türkiye, there lived 
a curious little cat named Karamel. 
Karamel loved two things more 
than anything else: adventures and tasty 
treats. 
One day, she found a colorful map 
with a sparkling path that wound through 
many different lands within Türkiye. 
“This must be a treasure map!”
Karamel meowed excitedly. 
She decided right then and there to follow 
the map and taste every special treat it led 
her to.
