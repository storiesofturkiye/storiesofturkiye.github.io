Karamel the cat lived in a sunny place called Türkiye. Karamel loved two things very much: having adventures and eating yummy treats!. One day, she found a colorful map. The map had a sparkly path that went through many different places in Türkiye. "Wow!" meowed Karamel. "This must be a treasure map!". She decided right away to follow the map. She wanted to taste every special treat the map showed her where to find.
4
5
A Crunchy Start in Istanbul. Karamel began her journey in the busy streets of Istanbul. The air smelled sweet, like "simit." Simit weren't just any bread rings. They were golden and covered in sesame seeds, and they made a lovely crunchy sound with every bite. “What a yummy start!” Karamel purred.
6
7
Olive Adventures in the Aegean.. Next, the map showed Karamel the sunny Aegean coast. This coast is very famous for its silvery olive trees! She tried something called "zeytinyağlı dolma." These were grape leaves filled with rice and cooked in olive oil, and they looked so shiny! It was like tasting the sunshine itself!
8
9
Fishy Tales in the Mediterranean.. Karamel sailed south and arrived at the Mediterranean Sea. There, she discovered "şakşuka," which was like a yummy dance of cooked vegetables and fresh fish. The fish were so fresh, it looked like they jumped right into the pot!
10
11
Dumpling Delights in Central Anatolia.. In the heart of Türkiye, Central Anatolia had a special food called "mantı." These little dumplings were like tiny presents! Each one was filled with yummy meat and topped with creamy garlic yogurt. Karamel couldn't stop at just one mantı!
12
13
Stormy Snacks in the Black Sea.. As the clouds gathered, Karamel found herself in the stormy Black Sea region. Here, "hamsi" was the star. These tiny fish were prepared in every way imaginable: baked, fried, and even in bread!
14
15
Mountain Meals in Eastern Anatolia.. Karamel climbed the rough mountains of Eastern Anatolia. She found something yummy called "kavurma." It was a warm lamb stew that made her feel cozy all over. The people there also shared their "eriste." It was homemade pasta, just right for a cold night!
16
17
Spicy Surprises in Southeastern Anatolia.. Finally, Karamel arrived at her last place: Southeastern Anatolia, a land full of yummy spices and delicious sweets! Here, she tried "baklava," which is a pastry. It was so sweet and rich, like taking a bite of a fluffy cloud when the sun goes down.
18
19
Karamel was full of yummy food and felt so happy! She understood that the best thing she found wasn't just all the delicious things she ate, but all the friends she made while exploring. She went back home, thinking about her next exciting trip in the amazing country of Türkiye.
