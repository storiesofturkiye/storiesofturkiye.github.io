Karamel, the adventurous little cat, heard about the seven wonders of Türkiye. Intrigued, she decided to embark on a journey to explore these magnificent sites. With a map in her paw and excitement in her heart, she set off on her new adventure.
4
5
Cappadocia Karamel's first stop was Cappadocia. Cappadocia is a special place with amazing fairy chimneys and fun hot air balloon rides! She looked at the strange and beautiful landscape and had a wonderful balloon ride. She saw the breathtaking views all around.
6
7
Pamukkale.. Next, Karamel went to Pamukkale! Pamukkale is very famous for its beautiful white steps and warm water. She put her paws in the warm water, which felt good, and looked around at the old ruins of Hierapolis.
8
9
Mount Nemrut Karamel then traveled to Mount Nemrut, where she saw the gigantic stone heads of ancient gods. She watched the sunrise from the mountaintop, feeling the mystical aura of the place.
10
11
Ephesus. In Ephesus, Karamel explored the grand ancient city, amazed by the Library of Celsus, the Great Theatre, and many other magnificent buildings. These buildings showed stories from a long time ago.
12
13
Hagia Sophia Karamel's next stop was Hagia Sophia in Istanbul, a magnificent building that has been both a church and a mosque. She looked at the amazing design of the building and thought about all the important things that happened there a long, long time ago. The history felt like it was whispering from the walls.
14
15
The Blue Mosque Karamel then visited the Blue Mosque, also in Istanbul, which is famous for its beautiful blue tiles and amazing buildings. She walked around its peaceful yards and looked at the graceful domes and tall minarets.
16
17
Sumela Monastery.. Karamel’s last place to visit was the Sumela Monastery. It was built on top of a tall cliff near the Black Sea. Karamel was amazed by how beautiful the monastery looked, high up on the mountain. She also loved the colorful paintings inside, called frescoes. The ancient place felt very peaceful and calm.
18
19
Karamel returned home with a heart full of amazement and her mind filled with all the wonderful things she saw in Türkiye. She understood that Türkiye's real treasures were its amazing stories from the past and its beautiful places to see, making her trip something she would never forget.
