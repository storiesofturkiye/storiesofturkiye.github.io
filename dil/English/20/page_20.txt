The Little Star and the Big Wind.
Once upon a time, there was a little star. She lived high up in the sky with all her star friends. One day, the little star felt a big wind coming. The wind was very strong!.
“Oh dear,” said the little star. “I hope I can hold on tight!”.
The big wind blew and blew. It tried to push the little star away. The little star wiggled and jiggled, but she didn’t move from her place. She held on very, very tight!.
“I won’t let you blow me away!” said the little star bravely. “I am strong!”.
The wind blew harder and harder. But the little star kept holding on. Finally, the wind got tired. It stopped blowing so hard..
“Oh,” said the wind. “You are very strong! I can’t blow you away.”.
The little star smiled. She was so happy that she had held on tight. She winked at her friends and shone even brighter than before. And she knew that even though the wind was big and strong, she could be brave and strong too.
