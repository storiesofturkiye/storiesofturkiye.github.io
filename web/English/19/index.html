Once upon a time, there was a playful little cat named Karamel, who loved exploring different games and activities. One day, she decided to visit various playgrounds across Turkey to learn about the traditional games children play.
4
5
Turkey - Istanbul: Blind Man’s Buff Karamel's first stop was Istanbul, where she joined children playing Körebe, a game where one child is blindfolded and tries to catch the others. It was so much fun listening to the laughter and excitement.
6
7
Aegean Region – Izmir: Dodgeball Then, Karamel traveled to Izmir, where she joined a group of children playing Yakan Top. The children were agile, dodging and throwing balls with great energy.
8
9
Mediterranean Region – Antalya: The Stick Game In Antalya, Karamel discovered the game of Çelik Çomak. She watched as children skillfully hit a small stick with a larger one, trying to score points.
10
11
Central Anatolia – Ankara: Hopscotch Then Karamel traveled to Ankara, where she joined children playing Seksek. She hopped along the squares drawn on the ground, enjoying the playful challenge.
12
13
Black Sea Region – Trabzon: A handkerchief grab. In Trabzon, Karamel participated in Mendil Kapmaca. The children were fast and competitive, trying to grab the handkerchief first and run back to their team.
14
15
Southeastern Anatolia – Diyarbakır: Knucklebone Game Karamel’s next stop was Diyarbakır, where she watched children play Aşık Oyunu, a game using knucklebones. The children demonstrated great skill and strategy.
16
17
Eastern Anatolia – Ardahan: Birdirbir While exploring Eastern Anatolia, Karamel came across a group of children playing a traditional game called Birdirbir. Curious, she decided to join them. The game involved jumping over a crouched child and chanting "Birdirbir!" with each leap. Karamel’s playful spirit and agility made her a star of the game, much to the delight of the children.
18
19
With her heart full of joy from playing and learning about traditional games, Karamel realized how these activities brought children together. She returned home, dreaming of her next playful adventure.
